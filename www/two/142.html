142.TUUFA IH RAWLDONAK PUAIAH

Key-C Missa Sannak Hla

1.A thianghlim bik mi Pathian ih Tuufa, Leitlun ah a rung ṭum zo, Kanmah sualnak hmuah kha in tlen ih, Kumkhua nunnuam ngah dingin, Hi puai cu in rak rem sak zo.

CHO:   Tuufa rawl donak sungah, Kawhnak a ngah tu hmuahhmuah in, thluasuah thluasuahnak an ngah, Tuufa rawldonak puai sungah, Puai sung ih a ra mi hmuah in, Lungawi zet in kan feh ding.

2.A thianghlim bik mi Pathian ih tuufa, Ih puai sungah nan feh ah cun, A ei mi hmuahhmuah in thianghlimnak, Zaangfahnak thawn a kim in, Bawipa thawn ṭhen lo an kom aw.

3.A thianghlim bik mi Pathian ih tuufa, Hi leitlun ih khual tlawng mi pawl, Bawipa ih na pawlpi tuurun kha, Tha khohnak thawn kim ṭheh in, A dingmi lamzin thlun ter aw.
