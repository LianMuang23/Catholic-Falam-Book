149.JESUH PATHIAN BAWIPA

Key-F Missa Sannak Hla

1.Jesuh Pathian Bawipa, Ka duhnak tumbik, Keimah thinlung sungah, Ka rinsan asi.

CHO:   Jesuh, Jesuh ka duh ṭul bik Bawipa, Keimah thinlung sungah, Bawipa rung um aw, Keimah thinlung sungah, Bawipa rung um aw.

2.Thinlung thianghlim mi pawl, A duhnak tum bik, Na zaangfahnak thawn in, Kei run tiang ter aw.

3.Duh ṭul Bawipa tlun ah, Ṭhatnak cem thei lo, Bawipa tlun lungawinak, Ka lo rul cawk lo.

4.Duh ṭul Bawipa liannak, A nei bang tuk in, Bawipa ih thin sung ah, In run zaangfah thei.
