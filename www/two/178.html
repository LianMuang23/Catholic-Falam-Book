178.HNATUAN UH

Key-A Forhnak Hla

1.Lo sung ah hnaṭuan ding tampi aum, Fang thar a cu at tu mal te lawng um, Bawipa aw thawng in ṭuan zuam tu an um, A thu tiam bang in ṭuan an zal.

CHO:   Hnaṭuan uh, Hnaṭuan uh, Lungawinak in ṭuan cio uh, Bawipa in thanuamnak kha a pek leh ding, Ni tlak hlan tiangin ṭuan ko uh.

2.Fang khum nak cuam sung hmuah in khat ter uh, Lungawi le nunnuam phah in hla sak uh, Ni tlak in khua a thim hlan lo ṭuan uh, Thinlung tak in ṭuan cio uh si.

3.Hanṭuan a har ih ni sau hman seh la, A ṭuantu hmuah in laksawng co dingah, Bawipa in a hril mi pawl bawm dingah, Lungawinak ṭhabik a pek ding.

4.Vancung khua sungah nunnuamnak aum, Harsa in ṭuantu hrang co ding a um, Bawipa'n a sim bangin dawtnak a um, An hna ṭuan har nak a cem ding.
