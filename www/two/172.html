172.A HLO DINGMI RUN AW

Key-A Forhnak Hla

1.A hlo ding mi run aw, Thi ding mi bawm aw, Sualnak le thlan thim sung in rung suak aw, A palhmi kha hram aw, a bah mi tho aw, Jesuh rundamnak thu khi va sim aw.

CHO:   A hlo dingmi run aw, Thi ding mi bawm aw, Zaangfah thiam Jesuh a lo bawm ve ding.

2.Sualnak in sir mi hmuah Jesuh a hngak ko, Kir leh ding vak vai mi a hngak asi, Hram thiam nak in a hnen nem ten in lem zo, A zum cun a sual nak ngaithiam ding asi.

3.Mi thinlung sungah khin sualforhnak aum, Thihnak le siatnak in uk hman seh la, Duhdawtnak in a pom zaangfahnak a um, A siat mi kan thinlung rem sal awla.

4.A hlo ding mi run aw thi ding mi asi, Bawipa in thazaang cahnak a pek ding, Lam fiak hmun ah hruai uh zaangfah za an si, An hrangah Jesuh thihnak thu sim ding.
