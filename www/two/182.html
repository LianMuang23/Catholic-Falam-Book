182.RAWL KHAWMTU KHUIAH AN UM

Key-G Forhnak Hla

1.Sualnak lo sungah a ṭhami fangci, Khui ah so an um a khawmtu ding mi, Thuhman naamkawn thawn fangci khi at uh, Rawl cuam thleng hlan tiang cawl lo in ṭuan uh.

CHO:   Fangci cuam sungah thun bawm ding in, Rawl lukhum sunlawinak khi thang ṭhatpi in, Sualnak lo sung a um mi buh cu, Zo in a khawmpi ding fangtel cu.

2.Lamzin kiangkap hmuah feh in hawl uh si, Hrampi sau hman seh fang an um asi, Lam ṭhatnak hmun cu ṭhelh lo in hawl uh, Vancung khua nuam um ding in sawm cio uh.

3.Vanrang hnuai hmuahhmuah in mi va sawm uh, Leitlun hmuah hi fangthar bang in ruat uh, Tuan ding a tam ṭuantu a mal asi, A caancem ding hngak lo in zuam uh si.

4.Leitlun mi hmuah hnen Pathian thu sim uh, A zumtu mi hmuahhmuah cu khawm aw uh, Lo nei tu a tum tiang in ṭuan uh si, A sunglawi nuamnak nanmah ta asi.
