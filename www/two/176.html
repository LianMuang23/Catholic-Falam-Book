176.KHRIH LE A PAWLPI HRANGAH

Key-D Forhnak Hla

1.Khrih le a pawlpi hrangah hla sak uh, Kan Siangpahrang Bawipa a hmin thangṭhat cio uh, Mino nan thazang cahnak bangin ṭuan uh, Pathian uknak sungah fek te in ding uh.

CHO:   In run tu Khrih kan Bawipa, Kan hrang a thihnak kha, Kan Pawlpi a thi in a lei, Bawipa in thiang ter aw.

2.Khrih le a Pawlpi hrangah thlacam uh, A thinglamtah nitin te in phur cio uh si, A Thiang Thlarau cahnak kha nei cio uh si, A hna kha rinsan tlak in ṭuan cio uh si.

3.Khrih le a Pawlpi hrangah thlacam uh, Sui le ngun nan neih mi a hrangah pek cio uh, Kan neih mi lak ṭhabik sup lo in pek uh, Cu tluk siannak veel lungṭha kha va nei uh.

4.Khrih le a Pawlpi hrangah thlacam uh, Siatnak le hngalnak hmuah nan neih mi hlon cio uh, Kan nun sung can a hnaṭuannak kan hmang ding, Bawipa vorhlam ah lungawi in kan thleng ding.
