103.JESUH HNEN AH NA FEH ZO MAW?

Key-C Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Sual ngaithiam ding in Jesuh hnen na feh maw? Tuufa thi sen sung ah na kholh maw? A zaangfahnak cu kim te'n na rin san maw? Tuufa thi sen sungah na kholh maw?

CHO:   Tuufa te thi sung ah, Na sualnak thiang dingin na kholh maw? Na thlarau puan vur hnak in a var sawn maw? Tuufa thi sen sungah na kholh maw?

2.Nitin te in Runtu thawn na feh khat maw? Tuufa thisen sungah na kholh maw? Thinglamtah ah hnangam ten na cawl ṭheu maw? Tuufa thisen sungah na kholh maw?

3.Mopa thlen tikah na puan a var ding maw? Tuufa thi in na thian var ter maw? Na thlarau vancung luh ding a ralring maw? Tuufa thisen sungah na kholh maw?
