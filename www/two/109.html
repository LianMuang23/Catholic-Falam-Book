109.PAWLPI THUKHAM PARUK

Key-G Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Ulh ding in thla a kim tikah, Hnaṭuannak hmuah bang ṭheh in, Pathian duhnak thlun thei dingin, Missa rak ngai cio uh law.

CHO:   Catholic thu thlun mi hmuah in, Jesuh dungthlun pawl tuah mi, Pawlpi thukham hmuahhmuah tla khi, Kim zet in thlun tlang uh si.

2.Kanmah tuahmi sualnak tlun ah, Sirnak thinlung kan nei ding, Kumkhat voikhat a mal bik in, Sual thlah nan dil ringring pei.

3.Thosalnak puai a thleng tikah, Kan thlarau rawl asi mi, Bawi Jesuh ih Thi le a Sa, Upatnak thawn ei uh si.

4.Rawl ulh ni le sa ei ulh ni, A tikcu a thleng tikah, Nan thinlung khi sup hram uh law, A kim te in ul hram uh.

5.Nanmah thinlung duhnak bangin, Bawm theinak caan nan hmuh le, Pawlpi uktu Siangbawi tla khi, Bawm thei dingin rak zuam uh.

6.Pathian duhnak hmuh thei dingin, Nupi ṭhit puai tuahnak khi, Ulh ding ni thla a thleng tik ah, Puai tuahnak khi bang hram uh.
