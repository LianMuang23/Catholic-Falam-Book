164.A HMIN KO TU HMUAHIN

Key-F Dilnak Hla

1.Maw lungawinak thu ngai uh, Khuazakip ah than uh, An thah mi Jesuh thawng in runnak thuthangṭha cu, Pathian thuṭha kumkhua in a thukam bang thlun uh, Zumnak in Jesuh zoh tu'n lawm uh a hmin mawi cu.

CHO:   Runtu Jesuh hnen ah thla a cam tu hmuah in, A hmin ko tu hmuah in runnak kha an ngah ding, Runtu Jesuh hnen ah thla a cam tu hmuah in, A hmin ko tu hmuah in runnak cu an ngah ding.

2.Khuathim sungih a um mi, sualnak lam a thlun mi, Zaangfah tleunak cu zoh uh, Pathian tuufa zoh uh, Nan thinlung hmuah in zum uh, A thu kam bang asi, Amah hmin ko tu hmuah in a hmin cu thangṭhat uh.

3.Thilrit phur in a bang mi, Retheinak a tuar mi, Na thilrit ka sawng dingih, Nangmah lo tong dingin, Mi zapi in an hmuh mi, Laksawng man har pek mi, Amah hmin ko tu hmuah in, Amah zumtu hmuah in.
