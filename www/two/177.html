177.FEH IN TUAN UH

Key-C Forhnak Hla

1.Christian tho it nawn hlah uh, Mi ninghang an ṭah lai ah, Kut sih in to men hlah uh, Bawipa hna ṭuan fial tikah.

CHO:   Feh in ṭuan, a tu thok uh, Feh in hawl, Tuu hlo mi khi, Sualthim sung, A tang mi khi, Puainak hmun ah run hruai uh.

2.Runtu Bawi hmin kan duh maw? Kan lo zum asi kan ti, Naih mi pek ding na kam maw? A kawh aw ngai ding asi.

3.Kan zum zat in kan zum maw? In zirh bangin kan nung maw? Zamrang in a hnen pan aw, Ṭuannak in zumnak lang ding.

4.Thinglamtah hmun ra pan uh, Cross co tu ding kan si zo, Tha thar in hmai nor cio uh, Caan a tawi ni a nai zo.
