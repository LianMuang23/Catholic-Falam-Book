150.THI SUNGAH SUNGLAWINAK AUM

Key-A Missa Sannak Hla

1.Na sual phur rit sungin luat na duh maw? A thisen sungah cahnak aum, Sualnak sung in luat sal ding na duh maw? Thisen sungah cahnak aum.

CHO:   Mangbangza ngaingai cahnak aum, Tuufa thisen sungah, Mangbangza ngaingai cahnak aum, A sunglaw tuufa thi sungah.

2.Hngalnak le puarnak in luat na duh maw? A thisen sungah cahnak aum, Kholhfainak Calvary ah va pan aw, Thisen sungah cahnak aum.

3.Vur hnak in var sawn dingin na duh maw? A thisen sungah cahnak aum, Sualnak hmuah kholhfai tu nunnak ti cu, Thisen sungah cahnak aum.

4.Na siangpahrang Jesuh cu na rian maw? A thisen sungah cahnak aum, Nitin thangṭhatnak hla sak na duh maw? Thisen sungah cahnak aum.
