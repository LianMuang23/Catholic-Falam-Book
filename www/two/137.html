137.NUNNAK SANGREU

Key-F Missa Sannak Hla

1.Jesuh in lam in hmuh kanmah tla hrangah, Kumkhua nunnak ka pek ding tin thu a kam, Amah in lam in hruai kanmah tla hrangah, Duhnak thawn a nunnak in pek.

CHO:   Keimah cu Nunnak Sang, Vancung in ṭum mi Sangreu, Hi sangreu a ei tu, Kumkhua in nunnak an ngah ding.

2.Zonzaih thilrit phur pawl ka hnen ah ra uh, Nunnak le colhdamnak nan hnen ka pek ding, Lungsiatnak retheinak kan thilrit hmuahhmuah, Jesuh hnen ah kan ret ṭheh ding.

3.Kumkhua nunnak lamzin kan zawh lai fangah, Ṭihnungza thlisia pi in in hran ringring, Thanau le ṭap phah in Bawipa kan ko ih, Nunnak sangreu thawn in in run.
