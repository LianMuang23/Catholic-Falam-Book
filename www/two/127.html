127.THIANGHLIM BIAKTHENG HMAIAH

Key-G Thawinak Hla

1.Thianghlim biakṭheng hmai ah, Lungawinak thawn kan khawm asi, Thin khat lung khat te in, Na hnen sumnak kan pek asi.

CHO:   A thianghlim biakṭheng ah sumnak kan pek, Na duhmi fate ih lak sawng pawl cu, In sang aw Bawipa kan Bawipa Pathian, Kan summi laksawng hi in sang aw.

2.Thianghlim biakṭheng hmai ah, Na Zaangfahnak thluasuah pek aw, Na zaangfah rul dingah, Kan pek mi sumnak in sang aw.

3.Thianghlim biakṭheng hmai ah, Sang le Sabit ti kan keng ih, Tlangbawi thawn kan kom ih, Bawipa na hnen kan sum asi.
