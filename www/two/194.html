194.THUANTHU SIM DING KA NEI

Key-F Forhnak Hla

1.Vanrang hnuaiah thuanthu sim ding ka nei, Lamding ah in hruai ding asi, Zangfahnak thuanthu ṭha ka nei, Remnak le tleu nak asi, Remnak le tleunak asi.

CHO:   Thimnak ram hmuah in avang ter ding, Sun ni tlangbang in a tleu ding, A sunglawi Khrih uk nak a thleng ding, Dawtnak tleunak asi ding.

2.Miphun kip hnen ah sak ding hla ka nei, Bawipa hnen lungawi ter ding in, Siatnak hmuah nehtu ding ka nei, (Fei le nam hman siat ding in)2

3.Thuanthu tha ramkip ah sim ding ka nei, Vancung Pathian uknak asi, Misual tlen ding Jesuh ka nei, (Pathian dawtnak a langter)2

4.Vanrang hnuai mi hmuhding Bawipa kan nei, Ninghannak mi hnem tu asi, Amah in mi run nak a nei, (Pathian ṭhat zia thei ding in)2
