147.BAWIPA JESUH KANMAH IN DUH

Key-A Missa Sannak Hla

1.Bawipa Jeush in in duhdawtnak cu, Ṭongkam thawn cun ka sim cawk lo, Bawipa Jesuh zumtu hmuahhmuah cu, Vancungram nunnuamnak kan ngah ding.

CHO:   Bawipa ka duh theinak dingin, Maria thinlung nei ningla, A tam sin in ka zuam thei ding, Bawipa Jeush ka duh thei ding.

2.Vancung in ṭummi nunnak sangreu, Aw Bawipa nang ka lo biak asi, Kan thinlung sung hmuahhmuah na rung tleu, Kan thlarau vur hnak var sawn asi.

3.A zumtu hmuah in nan thinlung khi, Bawipa umnak ah thianghlim ter ding, Kan thlarau hrang rawl ṭha bik asi, Kumkhua nunnak ram in thlen pi ding.
