200.AW THAWMTUMBIK ALLELUIA

Key-D Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Aw thawmtum bik Alleluia, Kan lo thangṭhat Imanuel, Thangṭhatnak le uknak hmuahhmuah, A kumkhua nangmah ta asi.

CHO:   Alleluia thluasuahnak um hram seh, Alleluia thluasuahnak um hram seh.

2.Leilung tlun mi phun hmuahhmuah in, Vancung mi thawn thinlung khat in, Runtu Bawipa kan lo thangṭhat, Van sang bik ah a hmin thang seh.

3.In duhdawtnak a lam dang ee, A fimnak le in duhdawtnak, Cherubim le Serafim in, In duhdawtnak an sim cawk lo.

4.Ra cio uh law, Thawmtum Bawipa, Caw rawl kuang ah puan nem te in, An fun ih Bawipa cu an hlum, In duhdawtnak ziang tluk a tum.
