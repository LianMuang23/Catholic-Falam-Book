118.BAWIPA BIAKTHENG HMAIAH

Key-E Thawinak Hla

1.Kan Pathian biakṭheng hami ah, Lungawi in kan naih asi, Kan sualnak hmuah a hmai ah, Ngaithiam ding kan dil asi.

CHO:   Pa Pathian, thangṭhat uh, A thawmtum Bawipa Pathian, Thangṭhat uh, Pa Pathian, Kannih kan thangṭhat ve ding.

2.Calvary ah Bawi Jesuh, A ruang pumpi a sum bang, Siangbawi sum mi sumnak cu, Jesuh sumnak thawn a bang.

3.Bawi thum kom Pathian hnen ah, Hminthan thawmtumnak um seh, Khuk bil in kan biaknak cu, Lungawi rulnak in si seh.
