160.JESUH THI LAWNG IN A THIANG THEI

Key-G Missa Sannak Hla

1.Ka sualnak ziang in thiang maw? Jesuh thi lawng in a thiang thei? Keimah dam ter tu um maw? Jesuh thi lawng in a dam thei.

CHO:   A sunglawi thlet mi thi, Vur bangin var ter tu, Ka tong lo ding cerhti, Jesuh thi lawng in a thiang thei.

2.Keimah thiang ternak ding ah, Jesuh thi lawng in a thiang thei, Keimah sual thlahnak dingah, Jesuh thi lawng in a thlah thei.

3.Ka sualnak ai tuar dingah, Jesuh thi lawng in a thiang thei, Laksawng ṭha ngahnak dingah, Jesuh thi lawng in a ngah thei.

4.Ka nunnak fek ter dingah, Jesuh thi lawng in a fek thei, Keimah miding si dingah, Jesuh thi lawng in a ding thei.
