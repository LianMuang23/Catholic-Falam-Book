236.KAN THIH TIKAH

Key-G Hnemnak Hla

1.Kan thih tik ah kan thih tik ah, Hi leitlun kan taan ding asi, In sim cia thihnak thukham in, Zo khal pelh lo in kan thi ding.

CHO:   Thih tik ah thih tik ah, Hnaṭuan nak a cem ding, Bawipa Pathian Bawipa Pathian, Sual nei mi tla thu ṭhen dingin.

2.Pangpar tla a vuai ol bangin, A rei hlan ah kan thi ding ih, Thihnak cu ralring lo lai ah, Rukfir tla bang in a thleng ding.

3.Mithi kiang ah nan rak naih pei, Hngalnak thinlung khi nan hlon ding, Leitlun nuamnak thilri hmuahhmuah, A zaten zianghman asi lo.

4.Nu le pa le rualpi tla khal, Thih tik ah an lo taan thluh ding, Um daan ṭha le ṭha lo nak lawng, Pathian hmai ah a lo hruai ding.
