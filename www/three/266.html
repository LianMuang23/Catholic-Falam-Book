266.LUNGAWI IN KA THANGTHAT

Key-F Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Ka thlarau lungawi in Maria a thangṭhat, Rei lo ten sui khuapi ah ka hmu ding asi, Ka thlarau lungawi in Maria a thangṭhat, Rei lo ten sui khuapi ah ka hmu ding asi.

CHO:   Sui khuapi, Vancung ah, Ka Bawinu, Ka hmu ding, Sui Khuapi, Vancung ah, Ka Bawinu ka hmu ding.

2.Pathian in sui khuapi cu rem le mawi te in, Maria cuih hmun ah feh in ka hmu leh ding, Pathian in sui khuapi cu rem le mawi te in, Maria cuih hmun ah feh in ka hmu leh ding.

3.Leitlun ah har le ṭih nak thawn a khat asi, Vancungram asi le harnak a um nawn lo, Leitlun ah har le ṭih nak thawn a khat asi, Vancungram asi le harnak a um nawn lo.

4.Ni tlang bang a tleu mi Bawinu Maria cu, Cuih sui khuapi ah kumkhua ka hmu ringring ding, Ni tlang bang a tleu mi Bawinu Maria cu, Cuih sui khuapi ah kumkhua ka hmu ringring ding.
