267.MAY THLA BAWINU

Key-E Bawinu Maria Hla

1.May thla sung Maria khi, Upat in lawm uh si, Zumnak nei mi hmuahhmuah in, Kan Bawinu thangṭhat uh.

CHO:   May thla sung Bawinu hnen ah, Hmui mi pangpar pe uh, Thlarau thinlung a hnen ah, A za ten kan pek ding.

2.May thla sung Bawinu hnen ah, Upat in lawm uh si, A mawi zet mi pangpar tla, Zuam zet in kan rem pei.

3.Thianghlim biakinn pi sungah, Hla thawn kan thangṭhat pei, Leitlun hmuah zel ṭheh dingah, Na pi'n kan thangṭhat pei.
