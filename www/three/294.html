294.MIPHUN HMUAHHMUAH

Key-F Hla Phunphun

1.Miphun hmuahhmuah Pathian hnen ah, Lungawinak tum zet thawn lawm uh, Pathian duhnak thukhamnak khi, (Thanuamnak thawn in thlun uh law)2

2.Kumkhua ih um Pathian lawng in, Kan Bawipa le Pathian asi, Thawmtum Bawipa Pathian hnen ah, (Lungawinak thawn in hla sak uh)2

3.Bawi Pathian in kanmah hmuahhmuah, A tuah ṭheh ih in kil ringring, Kanmaih Bawipa Pathian hnen ah, (Lungawinak thawn in hla sak uh)2

4.Mi hmuahhmuah in Pathian minung, Lungawinak thawn in hung khawm uh, Biakinn sung in Pathian tlun ah, (Aw khat tlang in hla sak uh law)2
