237.SUI KHUAPI

Key-G Hnemnak Hla

1.Thihnak va ral khat ah khin sui khua mawi a um, Pathian sunlawinak ramnuam sungah, Ni tlaknak a um lo, thinghnah hring mawi a um, A mawi zet mi sui khuapi sungah.

CHO:   Ni tlak ti a um lo, thinghnah an vuai ve lo, Zumnak nei mi hmuah in, Bawipa kha an hmu ding, A mawi zet mi sui khuapi sungah.

2.In tlentu Siangpahrang ka duh mi ka hmu ding, Mi thianghlim pawl thawn in cuih hmun ah, Khi khua ah a thiangmi arsi bang an tleu ding, A mawi zet mi sui khuapi sungah.

3.Thinglamtah nak hram ah mi tampi kha hruai uh, Lam palh mi hruai uh tuu dum sungah, A man har zet mi lungvar lukhuh kha khum uh, A mawi zet mi sui khuapi sungah.
