205.LAWMNAK HLA KAN LUNG SUNG AUM

Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Keimah lung sungah tleunak aum, Sunglawi tleunak asi, Ni tleu hnak in tleu sawn a um, Jesuh tleunak asi.

CHO:   Mawi hi lawmnak, Sunglawi lawmnak, Thlangam lungawinak a lang tikah, Jesuh hmai ṭha ka hmuh tikah, Ka lung sung lawmnak aum.

2.Hla mawi zet ka lung ah a um, Bawipa thangṭhatnak ah, Ṭong in ka sim thiam lo mi hla, Jesuh in a thei ko.

3.Thinghram hnah a cerh sal bangtukin, Ka lung thleng nak a um, Sal luat lungawinak bangtuk in, Ka lung lawm nak a um.

4.Tu ni in kei ka thinlung sungah, Nuamnak a khat asi, In pek cia le in pek lai ding, Na thluasuahnak asi.
