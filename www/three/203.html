203.PATHIAN CU THANGṬHAT UH

Key-Gb Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Bawi Khrih thawng in ra cio uh law u le nau, Kan aw neih miin Pathian cu, thangṭhat cio uh, Leitlun kan nun sung ah, Leitlun harnak hmuahhmuah tan ta uh law, Lungawinak thawn Pathian kan thangṭhat pei.

CHO:   Bawi Khrih hrang ah ṭuan cio uh si, Nu tar pa tar fala tlangval hmuah in, Kan pumkhawmnak caan sung hmuahhmuah khal ah, Kan Pathian in him ten in kil khawi seh.

2.Hlanlai kan pi pu sunlawinak tanta in, Thinlung thar le nunnak thar thawn, Thangṭhat cio uh in rak run tu Bawi cu, Lawm cio uh si Khrihfa ralkap mi pawl, A duhnak cu kumkhua kan hrang asi.

3.Hi leitlun kan nun sung harnak phunkip tla, Pathian rinsan in thinnuam ten, Hnangamnak cu Pathian in rak bawm seh, Ziang har lo ten tikcuu a kim ni tiang, Thin nau lo ten Pathian kan thangṭhat pei.
