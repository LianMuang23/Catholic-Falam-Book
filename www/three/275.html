275.ITTHAT DING FEH HLANAH

Key-G Zanlam Hla

1.It that ding feh hlan ah kan inn sungah, Pathian na fa te khukbil in, Kan thinlung na hnen ah kan sum, Tuih sun sual kan tuah le thlah aw Bawipa.

2.Khuathim lak tawivak tu kan ral khuavang khi, Hi tawk ih sin rak dawi hlo aw, Kan thlarau tla khi kilkhawi aw, Thianghlim tuukhal tu pa Bawipa Jesuh.

3.Kan duh mi minung hmuahhmuah kilkhawi aw, Zonzaih harnak ih sin run aw, Misual tla hnen ah zaangfahnak, Mithi tla hnen nuamnak pek aw Bawipa.
