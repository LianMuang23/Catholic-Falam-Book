208.MAW KA BAWIPA KA PATHIAN

Key-A Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Bawipa hnaṭuannak cu khuaruah har asi, A kut in lei le thil phunkim a tuah, Zan arsi tleu mawi le khuari aw thawng in, Na thawmtumnak leitlun ah an lang ter.

CHO:   In runtu Bawipa hla in ka thangṭhat, A thawmtum bik kan Bawipa cu, In runtu Bawi hla in ka thangṭhat ding, A thawmtumbik ka Bawipa cu.

2.Bawi Pathian in a Fapa neihsun te cu, Kanmah hrang harsat tuar dingin a thlah, Thinglamtah ah kan sual thilrit a phur zo, A thisen in kan sualnak a kholh zo.

3.Mumek tum in keimah in ko sal tikah, Ka thinlung cu lawmnak thawn a khat ding, Thinniamnak thawn Bawipa cu ka biak ding ih, A sunlawinak ka thangṭhat ding asi.
