273.LAWMNAK HLA SAK UH

Key-G Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Lawmnak thawn hla sak aw, Aw keimah ih thlarau, Rei lo in vancung ah, Maria ka lawm ding.

CHO:   Vancung, vancung, vancungram Bawinu khi, A rei hlan ah, Ka thangṭhat ding asi.

2.Pathian tuah mi khuapi, Vancung nunnuam ram ah, Bawinu Maria khi, Ka thangṭhat ding asi.

3.Thinphan zonzaih har nak, Leitlun a kim asi, Vancungram asi le, Riahsiatnak a um lo.

4.Ni tleunak bang tuk in, Bawi bik Nu Maria, Vancung nunnuam ram ah, Kumkhua in ka hmu ding.
