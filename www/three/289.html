289.BAWIPA KAN HNENAH TUM AW

Key-G Inn Thluasuah Peknak Hla

1.Runtu Bawipa kanmah hnenah rung ṭum aw, Nangmah a zum le rinsan mi innsang hnen ah, Satan ih kaih mi pa na dawi sak bangtukin, Hi innsung um Satan pawl khal in rak dawi sak ve aw.

CHO:   Lungawi uh, Bawipa hnen lungawi uh, Thazaang suah in hmailam nor sin uh, Jesuh in Satan kha a dawi sak ṭheh ding.

2.Runtu Bawipa hi innsung ah rung ṭum aw, Na vel bawmnak phunkim in in run pek hram aw, Na thukam bang vancungram kan thlen theinak ding, Abraham thluasuah na pek bangtuk in in pek ve aw.

3.Runtu Bawipa hi innsung ah rung ṭum aw, Job lungfeknak le hnangamnak na pek bangin, A thianghlim innsang Jesuh, Maria, Joseph bang, Duhnak daihnak le damnak thluasuah hi in pek hram aw.
