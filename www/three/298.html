298.KA KUT LAWNG IN KA KAI DING MAW

Key-D Hla Phunphun

1.Ka kut lawng in ka kai ding maw? Runtu Bawi tongnak dingah, Ni khat hman amah hrang ṭuan lo, Ziang ka pek lo keimah in.

CHO:   Ka kut lawng in ka kai ding maw, Runtu Bawipa tong dingah, Rualpi pawl hman sawm ta lo in, Ziang tin Bawipa ka tong ding.

2.Ka thih ding tla ka thin phang lo, Jesuh in in run ruangah, Rualpi sawm lo in ka hmu ding, Lungawi in ka um ko ding.

3.Nitin te in ka sual ringring, Cuih hrangah ka hnangam lo, Jesuh thu in um ka duh zo, A kumkhua in thlun ka duh.

4.Aw lung thiangmi it hlah, tho aw, Jesuh hrangah hna ṭuan aw, Thihnak ni a thlen hlan ah hin, Mi dang khal va forh ve aw.
