206.KA ZUMNAK IN KA THEI

Key-G Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Bawi Jesuh thu hla rei khawh thei lo cu, Vanmi le mi thianghlim pawl, Le mifim pawl thawn cibai an run buuk, Ka zumnak in ka thei.

CHO:   A duhdawtnak in zir ter, Lungawi ten ka thlun ve ding, A felnak tuih ni tiang in, Ka zumnak in ka thei.

2.Dam lo phun kim pawl dam ternak a nei, Kebai pawl feh thei nak thawn, Ti par ah ke ih feh ding in zir ter, Ka zumnak in ka thei.

3.Zonzaih le vakvai le hlawhsam pawl hrang, Galile ram ṭhing ah khan, Minung thangnga ei cawk lo in a zem, Ka zumnak in ka thei.

4.Kanmah pawl hrang ah hling lukhuh tiang in, Thinglamtah a tuar lai khan, A rat sal tiang in ngaithimnak a nei, Ka zumnak in ka thei.
