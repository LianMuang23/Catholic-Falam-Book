279.ZOHMAN IN THEN THEI LO

Key-Bb Thitumnak Hla

1.A hram thok ih sin Bawipa'n, Nunau le mi pa a tuah, Cu mi an pahnih nak in, Thi khat sa khat a cang ter, Nu le pa kop khat suak ter, Zo hman in ṭhen thei lo.

CHO:   (Zo hman in, ṭhen thei lo)2 Zo hman in ṭhen ter hlah seh, Pathian ih kom ter mi cu, Zo hman in, ṭhen thei lo.

2.Nunau in amah uk lo, A pasal in a rak uk, Cu mi an pahnih in, An pasal duhnak thlun in, Inn sungsang nunnuam ngah ding, Khat le khat duh aw in.

3.Mi pa khal in a nupi tlun ah, Zaangfah nak nei in, Zonzaihnak ton tikah, Amaih pumhrek a cang ih, A nupi ṭha ten kil seh, Pathian duhnak ruat in.
