207.DUHDAWTNAK SIM THEI LO

Key-G Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Runtu Bawi duhdawtnak thuthang ṭha, Ka theih in lungawi in ka mitthli kha a luang, Sual rethei nung tlak lo, Rundamnak run keng in nunnak cem thei lo mi in run pek.

CHO:   Thangṭhat uh in runtu duhdawtnak, Duhdawtnak ruangih a tuarnak cu, Sual rethei nung tlak lo rundamnak run keng in nunnak cem thei lo mi in run pek.

2.Aw cu tluk duhdawtnak thuk mi le thawmtum cu, Caw rawl kuang le Calvary thleng in, Duhdawtnak sim thei lo, Lei le van ah a khat, Kei ka thangṭhat khop thei ding maw si.

3.Kei misual hawl dingin, Van inn nuam tanta in, Leitlun harnak lak ah a vakvai, Duhnak ruang in a au, Thinglamtah tuar lai ah, Misual hmuahhmuah hrang nunnak a pek.

4.Kumkhua in in duhdawt a ṭang sungah in pom, Riahsia mangbang in ka um lai ah, Ka riahsiatnak hmuah le ka harnak i tuar pi, A duhdawtnak cun i run hnem ṭheu.
