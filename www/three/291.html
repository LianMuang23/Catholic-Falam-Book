291.TIPI TILET

Key-A Mithianghlim Pawl Hla

1.Thlipi tilet tho tikah, Nang kan lo pan asi, Joseph kan thinphan caan ah, In rak kil tu na si.

CHO:   Duh ṭul Joseph, Thawmtum mi pa, Kan Pawlpi in rak kilkhawi aw.

2.Aw Joseph thu hman bik mi, Maria thawn kom in, Ṭihnungza thli pi ruah pi, In run aw har nak in.

3.Nau no te Jesuh tlun ah, Pa duhnak si seh law, Na thuhman le ṭhatnak ah, Kanmah khal in pek aw.
