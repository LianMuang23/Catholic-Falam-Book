286.BAWIPA PATHIAN KAN THANGTHAT

Key-Bb Inn Thluasuah Peknak Hla

1.Bawipa Pathian kan thangṭhat, Kan lawmnak na hnen thleng seh, Hih inn minung kut sak mi, Na biakinn ah rak cang seh.

CHO:   Kan lo ap asi Bawipa, Na hmin thianghlim theinak ding, Aw Bawipa, In um pi aw, Na hrang ah thianghlim ter aw.

2.Hi innsung ra mi hmuahhmuah, Thluasuah famkim in pek aw, Tukforhnak le Satan ral, Nehnak thazaang nei dingin.

3.Bawipa na inn thianghlim hi, Ka duh bik ka thinlung ah, Thlarau rethei baang mi hrang, Lawmnak hmun sunglawi cang seh.
