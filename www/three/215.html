215.MITHIANG UMNAK HMUN

Key-Bb Hnemnak Hla

1.Thianghlim mi pawl umnak van Jerusalem khua thar ah, Ni tlang tleu bang puan rang thiangfai zet hruk in an um tlang, Thawmtum Pathian Bawi hmai ah lawmnak hla sak in an um tlang ṭheu, Nitin te in cuih khua mawi ah sui sangkaa cu, Khar kel dah lo mi mi thianghlim pawl luhnak hmun khua.

2.Cuih khuapi mawi sungah nomnak phunkim thawn an um tlang, Lungsiatnak le zonzaihnak phunkim tla khi an neh zo, An nehnak hla sak in puai an tuah Jerusalem khua thar ah, Nehnak laksawng sui lukhuh khum in an um tlang, Sui tleu bangtuk puan rang hngin hruk in hla an sak tlang.

3.Leilung a cem tikah vancung darkhing khi tum dingin, Mi fapa cu thawmtumnak thawn leitlun ah a ṭum ding, Mi thianghlim pawl lawng in thianghlim pawl umnak khua sung an lut ding, Thlarau hrangah nunnak laksawng man har bik cu, Ni bang in a tlang ding Jerusalem khua thar ah.

4.Cuih khua kan thleng tikah misual tla hrang thihnak asi, Taksa nomnak hmuahhmuah hlon uh la, Pathian lam zuam uh, Kan sualnak in thlarau nunnak lam ṭha in siatsuah ṭheu asi, Kan pial ding ih cuih sualnak lam kan hmuh tikah, Thianghlim pawl umnak hmun Jerusalem khi zoh uh.
