297.KUMKHUA NUNNAK NA DUH LE

Key-C Hla Phunphun

1.Ngai uh law Bawi Jesuh in a nun lai thu in cah mi, Kumkhua nunnuam na duh le, Na Pathian khi duh awla, Amah in a lo pek mi a thukham kim ter thlun aw, Midang pawl khal nangmah bang in na duh pei.

CHO:   Bawi Jesuh in sim cia zo bangin, Leitlun ah a fate kanmah in, Amah kha catloin kan rak hngak, A thlen tikah in ko ding kan Bawipa in.

2.Adam sualhrampi ruangah leitlun minung kan zaten, Pathian pek mi nunnuamnak, Satan hrangah a feh ṭheh, Sikhal sehla sualsir in, Baptism kan sang tikah, Kan thlarau cu Pathian in a zaangfah sal.

3.Baptism kan ngah hnu ah thianghlim te in um a ṭul, Sualforhnak khi neh lo in misual mi kan cang sal ṭheu, Cutikah Pathian hnen ah sualsirnak thawn kir sal in, Sual thlah dil in na thlarau cu na run pei.
