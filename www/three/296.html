296.THENHRA THENKHAT

Key-G Hla Phunphun

1.Bible ca pakhat ah, Pathian in a sim, Na neih mi hmuah sungah, Ṭhenhra ṭhen ṭhenkhat, Na pek ding tiah a sim, Amai dil mi cu, Kan leiba asi ih, Kan pek sal a ṭul.

CHO:   Kan suah pekte lai ah, Zianghman keng lo in, A tu na neih mi cu, Bawipa ta asi Pathian in mal in sawm, Kan nunsung hrangah, Cuih kan neih mi thil cu, Kan pek sal a ṭul.

2.Khua hlan pi san lai ah, Abel le Kaina, Pathian in cuih hnen ah, Ṭhenhra ṭhen ṭhenkhat, Unau pahnih te in, An neih mi laksawng Kaina a zangraam hrang ah a sang duh lo.

3.Hi unau hnih lak ah, Pathian a duh tu, Nan zaten ruat hnik uh, Abel ih laksawng, Lungawi te in a pe, Pathian in laksawng lungawi te in a sang, Na neih hmuah sup hlah.
