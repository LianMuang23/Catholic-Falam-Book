261.SUALHRAMPI IHSIN MARIA A THIANGHLIM

Key-E Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Maria sual hram pi ihsin a thianghlim, Amah ih nu sung in a thiannak ka zum, Hling nei mi kung lak ah daidim par var bang, Adam fate lak ah Maria a tleu, Hling nei mi kung lak ah daidim par var bang, Adam fate lak ah Maria a tleu.

2.Iksik, puarthau duhnak mit ṭhep khat te khal, Maria thinlung sung pakhat khal kop lo, Minung tla nu sungin sualhram an keng naan, Maria asi le sualnak a nei lo, Minung tla nu sungin sualhram an keng naan, Maria asi le sualnak a nei lo.

3.A thianghlim bik nu sualhrampi nei lo in, A kumkhua fala le Bawi Jesuh ih Nu, Vancungram in Bawinu leilung tlun zaangfah, Leitlun ral cang tu Rulpi lu khi pal aw, Vancungram in Bawinu leilung tlun zaangfah, Leitlun ral cang tu Rulpi lu khi pal aw.
