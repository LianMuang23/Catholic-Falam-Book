280.INNSUNGSANG DUHAWKNAK

Key-D Thitumnak Hla

1.Kan kiang dap hmuh a nuam, Innsang duhdawtnak um tikah, Ziang hmuah ah duhdawtnak, Ziangtluk sunglawi, innsang dawtnak.

CHO:   Inn sungsang duhnak cu nunnuamnak bik asi, Bawipa Pathian khal in in duhdawt ding, Inn sungsang remnak cu caan hmuahhmuah a nuam ih, Kan Pa Pathian khal in a lungawi ve.

2.Lo thlam te hman a nuam, Inn sang duhdawtnak um tikah, Hmun kip ah mithmai ṭha, Ziang tluk sunglawi innsang dawtnak.

3.Ṭongkam khal a thlum cuang, Innsang duhdawtnak um tikah, Hnaṭuan khal tha a nuam, Ziangtluk sunglawi innsang dawtnak.

4.Zonzaih riahsiat tuar tlang, Innsang duhdawtnak um tikah, Nunnuam khal ah nuam khat, Ziangtluk sunglawi innsang dawtnak.
