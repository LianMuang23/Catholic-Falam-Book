253.MAY THLA NI HLEITHUM AH

Key-G Bawinu Maria Hla

1.May thla ni hlei thum ah, Vancungram ih sin, Covada iria ah a ṭum Maria.

CHO:   Ave Ave Ave Maria, Ave Ave Ave Maria.

2.Tuukhal pawl hnen ah hin, Bawi Pathian Nu in, Amah thinlung zaten a theih ter asi.

3.Sual nei lo pawl thlah nak ding, An ngah thei nak ding, Sualsirnak tum bik thawn thla nan cam sak pei.

4.Cuih thianghlim Rosary, Zuam in nan cam le, Pathian in leitlun khi, Remnak a pek ding.

5.Thinlung niam nak thawn in, Kan thlacam nak khi, Rak ngai aw Maria, A thianghlim bik Nu.

6.Covada Iria ah, A rel bang tuk in, Leitlun rem ter nak ding, In ruat sak seh law.
