Rosary Thlacamnak

  1. Pa, Fapa, Thiangthlarau ih hmin in, Amen.
  2. Dungthlun pawl ih zumnak thlacamnak.
  3. Bawi Jesuh zirhmi thlacamnak
  4. Bawinu Maria hnen ih thlacamnak(voi-3)
  5. Bawi Pathum Pathian pakhat lomnak
  6. Rosary hmunkhat cam theh tinten, Thuthuk pa khat ruat in dil duhmi dil vivo ding asi. Rosary hmun hat cam theh tinten a tanglam ih thlacamnak hi cam ding asi.

Fatima Thlacamnak

Aw ka Bawipa Jesuh, kan sualnak in ngaithiam awla, Hellrampi thawn in pelh ter aw. Minung hmuahhmuah vancungram thleng ter awla, na zaangfahnak a cuangce in a tul pawl run bawm aw.

Rosary cam tikih ruah dingmi thuthuk(20)

Lungawinak thuthuk(Tlawngkai ni khat le zarhte ih ruah ding)

  1. Vancungmi Gabriel in Fala Maria hnen ih Thuthannak
  2. Bawinu Maria in Elizabeth hnen ih a lennak
  3. Bethlehem khua ah Naute Jesuh asuahnak
  4. Jerusalem Biakinn sungih Naute Jesuh an apnak
  5. Nithum hnu ah a nu le a pa ih Jerusalem biakinn sungih Bawi Jesuh an tonsalnak

Riahsiatnak thuthuk(Tlawngkai ni hnih le ni nga ih ruah ding)

  1. Gethsemane hmuan sungah Bawi Jesuh a thlan thisen luang in thla a camnak
  2. Thuam ah Bawi Jesuh an hreng ih hri hrual thawn an vuaknak
  3. Bawi Jesuh hling lukhuh an khum ter nak
  4. Bawi Jesuh Thinglamtah a phurnak
  5. Thinglamtah parih Bawi Jesuh a thihnak

Sunlawinak thuthuk(Zarhpini le tlawngkai nithum ih ruah ding)

  1. Bawi Jesuh Thihnak ih sin a thawhsalnak
  2. Bawi Jesuh Vancungram a kainak
  3. Dungthluntu pawl le Bawinu Maria hnenih Thiang Thlarau a tumnak
  4. Nu Maria Vancungram ih hruainak a ngahnak
  5. Nu Maria Vancungram ih Siangpahrang lukhuh a ngahnak

Tleunak thuthuk(Tlawngkai ni li ih ruah ding)

  1. Jordan tiva ih Bawi Jesuh tihnimnak a ngahnak
  2. Kanaan khua nupi thit puai ih Bawi Jesuh ih mangbangza a tuahnak
  3. Bawi Jesuh in Pathian uknak thuhla phuang in sualsir ding ah in kawhnak
  4. Bawi Jesuh ih hmuihmel thlengnak
  5. Bawi Jesuh in a Thi le Sa thawinak a dinnak

Hminsin:   : Adventu le Vua sungih zarhpi ni pawl khal ah Sunlawinak thuthuk pawl ruah ding asi.

Siangpahrang Bawinu Hnenih Thlacamnak

(Rosary 50 cam theh ih cam ding)

Kan nunnak, kan nuamnak le kan ruahsannak asimi, zaangfahnak neitu aw Siangpahrang Bawinu, cibai kan lo buk asi. Eva ih fate pawl in, zonzaih harsatnak le mithli tlaknak ram ihsin zaangfahnak kan lo dil asi. Na zaangfahnak mithmai tha thawn in run zoh hram aw. Zaangfahnak, duhdawtnak, thinnemnak le thatnak hmuahhmuah thawn a khat mi aw Bawinu, harsatnak ram ih sin kan luat hnu ah a sunglawi zet mi na Fapa Jesuh in hmuh hram aw.

V: Pathian ih Nu, Thianghlim Maria kanmah hrang ah thla in cam sak aw.

R: Kanmah khal Christu Bawipa ih thukamnak co tlak tu kan cang theinak dingin,

Thla kan cam pei;

A kumkhua ih a hmun mi Pathian Bawipa, Na Fapa neihsun Bawi Jesuh ih a thihnak le a thawhsalnak thawngin kanmaih hrangah kumkhua nunnuamnak laksawng in ngah sak asi. Hih thuthuk pawl ruat phah in Bawinu Maria ih Rosary kan camnak ihsin cuih thu thuk ih zirhmi nun daan tha le in kam cia mi pawl kan ngah theinak dingah na Fapa Jesuh Christu hmin in kan lo dil asi.Amen.

SALVE REGINA

Salve Regina mater misericordiae, Vita dulcedo, et spes nostra salve. Ad te clamamus, exsules, filli Hevae, Ad te suspiramus, gementes et flentes in hac lacrimarum valle. Eia ergo, Advocata nostra, illos tuos misericordes oculos ad nos converte. Et Jesum, benedictum fructum ventris tui, nobis post hoc exsilium ostende. O clemens. O pia. O dulcis, virgo Maria.

V: Pathian ih Nu, thianghlim Maria kanmah hrang ah thla in cam sak aw

R: Kanmah khal Christu Bawip ih thukamnak co tlaktu kan cang theinak dingin

Thla kan cam pei;

Aw Pathian Bawipa, vancungmi ih thuthannak in na Fapa Christu Bawipa ih minung cannak, lungawi nak thu a thei tu kanmahin, Bawipa ih zonzaih tuarnak le thinglamtah parih a thihnak ihsin a thosal sunlawi nak an thlen theinak dingin kanmah thinlung sungah zaangfahnak in pe aw tiah Bawi Jesuh rinsan in kan lo dil asi.Amen.

Vancung Siangpahrang Bawinu Hnenih Thlacamnak

V: Vancung Siangpahrang Bawinu lungawi aw. Alleluia

Zianghrangah tile na hrinmi Jesuh cu, Alleluia

V: A rel cianak bangin a thosal zo asi, Alleluia

R: Pathian hnen ah thla in cam sak aw, Alleluia

V: Lungawi aw Fala Maria, Alleluia

R: Zianghrangah tile Bawipa cu a ngaingai in a thosal zo asi, Alleluia

Thla kan cam pei;

Na Fapa kanmaih Bawi Jesuh Christu ih thosalnak in minung zate tla lungawi tertu aw Pa Pathian, a Nu Fala Maria ih bomnak in, kumkhua nunnak ih lungawinak kan ngah theinak dingin, cuih Fapa Christu Bawipa rinsan in kan lo dilnak khi in rak ngai aw.Amen.
