Credo (Hla in) P.Pathian pakhat lawng ka zum.
1.Lei le van hmuh theih, hmuhthei lo tuahtu, Thawmtumbik Pathian ka zum, Pathian Fapa neihsun Jesuh Khrih ka zum, Ziang hmuah tuah hlan in a suak.
2.Pathian in Pathian, Tleu-nak in Tleu-nak, Pathian ngai in Pathian ngai; Thawknak cemnak nei lo-in a suakih, Pathian thawn pumkhat an si.
3.Ziangkim a hnen in a suak ih, kan run, Damnak hrang Van in a ṭum, Thiang Thlarau thawngin Fala Maria, Sungin minung ah a cang.
4.Pilatus sanah thing par an thlai ih, A thi ih an phum asi, Nithumnak ah thlan sungih a thosal, Bible ih simcia bang in.
5.Vancungram ah sunlawinak thawn a kai, Pa ih vorhlam ah a to; Thuṭhen dingin a rungṭum sal dingih, A ram a cem dah lo ding.
6.Pa le Fa in a suak mi; Thiang Thlarau, Nunnak petu Bawi ka zum, An mah thawn bang rep in kan thangṭhat ih, Profet hmangin thu a sim.
7.A thianghlim pakhat, catholic Pawlpi, A thlah mi pawl thawn zom mi; Sualthlahnak hrang Tikholhnak, thawhsalnak; Kumkhua nunnak pawl ka zum.Amen.
