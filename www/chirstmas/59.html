59.BAWI JESUH A THOSAL

Key-C Thosal Hla

1.Bawi Jesuh a tho sal Alleluia, Khrih fa mi in lawm cio uh Alleluia, A thluntu pawl hla rem uh Alleluia, Van le lei in kan thangthat Alleluia.

2.Zaangfahnak thawn a kim ih Alleluia, Ral donak ah a neh zo Alleluia, Rulhreng cu a dawi theh zo Alleluia, Hmin thang hngin asi hrang ah Alleluia.

3.A hmun lungpi a thawn ih Alleluia, Hrem hmun sangkaa a boh theh Alleluia, Thihnak cu a neh theh zo Alleluia.

4.Runtu Bawipa a tum bik Alleluia, Kumkhua hremnak in a luat Alleluia, A tu zaangfahnak a thleng Alleluia, Kumkhua in kan nuam zet ding Alleluia.
