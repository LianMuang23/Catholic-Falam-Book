46.THINGLAMTAH AUM

Key-A Vua Hla

1.A hlapi tlangah thinglamtah aum asi, Ningzahnak reitheinak hmun asi, Hi leitlun rundamnak thinglamtahnak asi, Duhnungza an that kan Bawi asi.

CHO:   Cuih thinglamtah cu ka duh ngaingai, Ka nunnak hmuah a cem hlan tiangin, Ka hngaksan mi khi thinglamtah cu, Tui hnu sui lukhuh thawn thleng dingin.

2.Khi thinglamtah cu leitlun mi'n hmuhsuamnak in, Mangbangza kan thinlung zeltu cu, Pathian Tuufa cu vancung sunlawinak tan in, Calvary tlang ah thihnak tuar in.

3.Khi thinglamtah cu a kumkhua in ka zum ding, Ningzah hman ka tuar ngam ding asi, Vancung khua um ding i kawh tikah, Ka tlung ding, Amah sunlawinak zar ka co ding.

4.Khi thinglamtah cu manhar thiluannak asi, Ka hmuh tikah mangbangza ka ti, Khinah khin Bawi Jesuh thih tuarnak hmun asi, Ka sual thian ternak hmun thiang asi.
