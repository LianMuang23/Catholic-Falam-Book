10.BETHLEHEM ARSI TLEU MAWITE

Key-G Christmas Hla

1.Maw Bethlehem Arsi tleu mawi te, Lam hlapi in dim ten a tlee, Caan sau a vakvai mi tleunak a pe, Mifim pawl an fehnak lam a hruai, Jesuh umnak hmun lam pan in, Bethlehem Arsi tleu mawite vang aw.

CHO :   Amah thangthat le lawmnak hla sak uh, Leitlun buainak leh sualthim sungah remnak um seh an ti.

2.Tuukhal pawl le mifim pawl in, A hmaiah lu kun in an biak, An thlun zawnah Arsi tleu le, Vancungmi hla aw a zaam.

3.Cuih aw in ka lung i len ter, Ka thinlung hri bangin a ngerh, Arsi tleu pawl an liam thei lo, Van laiah an um lai hrih.

4.Ka sim thiam lo, Ka rel thiam lo, A thawmthat le a mawi daan cu, Siar thiam thei thiam si ningla maw, Nunnak a hnen pek a tlak.
