61.BAWIPA THIHNAK IN A THO

Key-Bb Thosal Hla

1.Bawipa thihnak in a tho Alleluia, Thihnak cu a rak neh zo Alleluia.

CHO:   Kan sualnak man a kuan theh zo, A thihnak in in tlen sak zo, Vancung sangkaa in rak on sak, Lungawinak in kan lut thei ding.

2.Mi sualnak khi thlah ding in Alleluia, Thinglamtah thahnak a tuar Alleluia.

3.Sualnak hrang a thi zo Alleluia, Thi luannak in in kholh sak Alleluia.

4.Rul hreng bumnak phoih dingin Alleluia, A thi ih thlan ah an phum Alleluia.

5.Khuathim lak kan um hrang ah Alleluia, A tho ih vur bang a tleu Alleluia.

6.Vancungram thlen kan duh le Alleluia, Amah dung in kan thlun pei Alleluia.
