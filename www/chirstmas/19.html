19.AW THUZUMTU PAWL TLA

Key-G Christmas Hla

1.Aw thu zumtu pawl tla, Lungawi zetnak thawn in, Ra uh law Bethlehem ah ra uh law, Nau te zoh dingin vanmi tla ih Bawi khi.

CHO:   (Ra uh law, kan biak khat pei)3 Bawipa khi.

2.Tuu rual pawl tan theh in, Tuukhal pawlzamrang in, Naute umnak thlam ah an naih theh asi, Cubangin kanmah lungawinak thawn naih ding.

3.Thawhnak cemnak nei lo, Kumkhua ih Pathian in, Ruang pumpi ah relh in aum asi, Puan fate sin mi Naute Jesuh Pathian.

4.Kanmah hrang farah tuar, Fang kuang tlun it mi khi, Duh zetnak thinlung thawn, Kan cawi khat pei, Cutluk ih duh ve tu duh ve lo zo um ding
