158.A SANNAK HMUN AH

Key-Ab Missa Sannak Hla

1.A sang bik sin thawmtum pa in, A fa neih sun a thlah asi, Thu in minung cannak lak in, Kanmah hnen ah a nung asi.

CHO:   Jesuh aw, Jesuh aw, Mangbangza Jesuh, In cawl ter aw maw, Nangmaih ṭang sungah.

2.Judah in Bawipa do dal in, Duhdawt vek in a hnam asi, Hi bang Bawipa duhdawtnak in, Thawinak a pek sang sabitti.

3.Milai taktak in a um ih, Vancung rawl thawn in cawm asi, In duh nak thi a luang ter ih, Kanmah hrangah in pek asi.
