196.RAWL KHAWMTU KAWHNAK

Key-G Forhnak Hla

1.A hlat le nai fing le tlang tin ah, Thlawh lai fang a thar zo, A hlat le nai khua zakip ah, Fang cang tha pawl a cu zo.

CHO:   Bawipa ka dilnak in ngai aw, Rawl nei tu Bawipa Jesuh, Fang attu dingin, Run thlah aw, Fang at can a thlen hlan ah.

2.Zing khuavang ve ten siseh, Fang at tu ding run thlah aw, Sun nitlak cem hlan ah siseh, Fangtel hmuah in khawmter aw.

3.Zamrang in fangtel kilkhawm ding, Fang cilnak hmunah khawm uh, Zanlamah Jesuh ara ding, Lungawi zetin tlungthlang uh.
