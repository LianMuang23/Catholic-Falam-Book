165.JESUH THU THEIH SINSIN KA DUH

Key-G Dilnak Hla

1.Jesuh thu theih sinsin ka duh, Zaangfahnak dil ka duh asi, Rundamnak thu ṭha thei ka duh, Keimah hrang ah a thi asi.

CHO:   Jesuh thu thei ka duh, Jesuh thu thei ka duh, Keimah hrang ah a thi nak thu, In runnak kha thei fiang ka duh.

2.Jesuh thu tampi thei ka duh, A thianhlimnak thei fiang dingin, Thiang Thlarau ih lam hruai ka duh, Christu Bawipaih ṭongkam in.

3.Jesuh cu ka thei fiang thlang ding, Ka Bawipa thawn um tlang ka duh, Amah thu kumkhua tlun ka duh, Mi hmuahhmuah khal thlun ter ka duh.

4.A umnak ka thei fiang thlang ding, Hminthannak thawn a khat asi, A ram cu a tum sinsin ding, Remnak Siangpahrang Bawi asi.
