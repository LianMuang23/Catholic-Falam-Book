159.NUNNAK IN PETU

Key-F Missa Sannak Hla

1.Nunnak in pe tu in tuah tu Bawipa, Na hla zo na in na sual tik khal ah, A lo duh ringring, kumkhua nunnuamnak, Nunnak sungbik cu a lo tlen sak zo.

CHO:   (Duhnak thawn) Thlacam in (Nunnak lam) hawl in feh (Bawipa hnen) Zumnak ro (co ding ah) a kumkhua (nunnuamnak) Lamzin kha (feh ding in) Ca thianghlim (Sung ngan cia) nunnak ro (sung bik cu) nangmaih hrang (asi).

2.Bawipa Pathian in, hawl in a lo hngak, Nangmaih sualnak lam, A ṭho lo mi pawl, Dung her in zoh aw, Ziangtik cuu fang in, A ra sal ding tin, A lo hngak ringring.

3.Pelh lo thu ṭhen ding, A kam cia bangin, Zoh aw Calvary, Thinghlamtah par ah, Zumnak nei mi pawl, Kumkhua vancungram, A zum lo mi pawl, Kumkhua Hell ram ah.
