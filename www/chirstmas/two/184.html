184.LEITLUN RAM HMUAHHMUAH AH

Key-A Forhnak Hla

1.Leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah, Nangmah thu thantu dingin, Leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah, Nangmah thu than tu thlah aw.

CHO:   Alleluia, Khuathim sung um mi pawl, Khua tleunak ngah dingin, Nangmah thu thantu pawl tleu ter aw mei bang in.

2.Chrisma thapeknak thawn, Ralthatnak khi pek in, Na ral do thiamnak thawn, Ral pawl in neh ter aw.

3.Lungsia retheinak in, Kan thinnau lai fang ah, In bawm aw vancung in, Hnangamnak co dingah.

4.Nangmah thei hrih lo tu, A dai thlang mi pawl in, Na hnen ah thlen dingin, Ka sim ding keimah in.

5.Leitlun ka nun sungah, Thinnuamnak in pe aw, Ka lo thlun na hnen ah, Na thu ṭha in pek aw.

6.Bawipa na thu hman cu, Mi tampi'n an thei lo, Ka sim ding na thu cu, Nang lawng ka rinsan zo.
