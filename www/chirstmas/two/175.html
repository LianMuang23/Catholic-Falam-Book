175.A HMANMI ZIN

Key-F Forhnak Hla

1.Leitlun ram kip ah minung tampi cu, Nunnak lamzin an thlun naan, Rualrem nei lo in, Mah lawng te an feh, Cuihcaan ah riahsiat an tong, Aw rual duh pawl tla uh, Thlarau sung sual lo ding, Sunlawinak Thuthang ṭha rak ngai thlang uh, Nang le kei hrang ah asi.

2.Nunnak lamzin ṭha ih in hruai tu cu, Jesuh ka Bawipa asi, A hnenah naih in a dung in thlun aw, Rual ṭha nei tu na si ding, Aw rualduh pawl tla uh, Jesuh hnen ah ra uh, Kumkhua ih nunnak a hman mi zin ah, A lo hruai ding hman te in.

3.Kan lamzin net vak, Vansui ramnuam ah, Kumkhua ih inn pi aum, Minung tuah si lo, Jesuh Bawipa in, A tuah zo, Duhdawtnak thawn, Aw rual duh pawl tla uh, Jesuh hnan ah ra uh, Kumkhua ih nunnak a hman mi zin ah, A lo hruai ding hman te in.
