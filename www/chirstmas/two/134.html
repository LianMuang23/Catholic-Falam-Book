134.DUHTUL JESUH PATHIAN BAWIPA

Key-D Missa Sannak Hla

1.Dulṭul Jesuh Pathian Bawipa, Keimah Bawi khi ka duh ngaingai, Sikhal seh la thin fek zet in, Ziang vek tluk in ka duh thei pei.

CHO:   Jesuh einak Sacramentu, Duh sawn dingin zuam ding asi, Jesuh einak Sacramentu, Duh sawn dingin zuam ding asi, Jesuh einak Sacramentu, Duh sawn dingin zuam ding asi, Jesuh einak Sacramentu, Duh sawn dingin zuam ding asi.

2.Nangmah ka duh theinak dingin, Maria thinlung nei ning la, Aw ziang vek tluk thinnuam zet in, Keimah Bawipa ka lawm zet hngin.

3.Minung lak ah Jesuh Bawipa, Na umnak khi mangbang asi, Bawipa Thi le Bawipa Sa khi, Kanmah hrangah rawl ah in do.

4.Jesuh taksa kan ei tik ah, Jesuh thawn baang lo kan kop aw, Kan ruat thei lo mangbangnak khi, A kumkhua in kei ka biak ding.
