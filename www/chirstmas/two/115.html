115.BAWIPA HNENAH PEK UHSI

Key-G Thawinak Hla

1.Leitlun kan nunnak a liam vivo, Ka rual aw caan ṭha te na neih lai ah, Kan tik cu le caan a liam zo, Na hmang ding asi Bawipa amah hrangah.

CHO:   Bawipa kan pek mi kan thilri hmuahhmuah tu ah, Na biakinn ah kan pek, Bawipa in pek mi kan sumsaw pawl cu, Bawipa in lungkim ten in sang ding.

2.Kan neih mi sum le saw pawl tla thawn, Bawipa thianghlim inn sungah kan feh ding, Ra aw na neih min hmuahhmuah thawn, Amah sungah lungawinak a khat sawn ding.

3.Bawipa in duhnak a kim asi, Ka sim rel thiam lo na duhdawtnak cu, Thingrah hmuah khal in pek asi, Lungawi ten kan pek pei, Kan neih mi hmuah cu.
