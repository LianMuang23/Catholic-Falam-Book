114.JESUH HMIN KENG UH

Key-G Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Jesuh hmin kha keng camcin uh, Lungsia retheimi hmuahhmuah, Lungawi hnangamnak a pek ding, Na fehnak kip ah keng ding.

CHO:   Hmin sunglawi, Cungnungbik, Lei le vancung lungawinak, Hmin sunglawi, Cungnungbik, Lei le vancung lungawinak.

2.Jeush hmin kha keng camcin uh, Nan ral phaw ah hmang cio uh, Sualforhnak in kulhvel hman seh, Jesuh hmin in thlacam uh.

3.Maw sunglawibik Jesuh hmin cu, Kan thlarau lung nuam ter tu, A duhnak ṭang sungah ben uh, Jesuh hmin in thlacam uh.

4.Jesuh hmin kha keng camcin uh, A ke hram ah kun cio uh, Vancung khua ih kan thlen tikah, Bawibik lukhuh khum dingah.
