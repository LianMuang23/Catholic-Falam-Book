179.FANGCANG TEL KHAWM IN

Key-C Forhnak Hla

1.Zing ah thlaici vorh uh, Vorh uh duhdawtnak thawn, Sun ih na phun bangin zan ah phun ve uh, Fangcang a thar zo ih a at ding kha hngak in, Fangcang tel khi khawm in lungawi in ra uh.

{Fangcang tel khawm in, Fangcang tel khawm in, Fangcang tel khi khawm in, Lungawi in ra uh}2

2.Ni tlaknak ah phun uh, Daihnem khal ah phun uh, Khuasik cuu ih mero kha va ṭih hlah uh, Rawl khawm caan a thleng ih ṭuancaan khal a cem in, Fangcang tel khi khawm in lungawi in ra uh.

3.Tap phah in va feh uh, Bawipa hrangah phun in, Harsatnak ruangah riahsiat in um hlah uh, Tahnak caan cem tikah, Bawipa in cawisang ding, Fangcang tel khi khawm in lungawi in ra uh.
