113.AMAH IN I HRUAI

Key-D Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Bawipa' kut in i hruai tikah, Ka lung a nuam amah hnenah, Ka umnak le ṭuannak hmun in, Amai' kut in i hruai camcin.

CHO:   Ama'n i hruai, Ama'n i hruai, Ama'i kut in keimah i hruai, Ama'i kut in i hruai ruangah, A dungthluntu ṭha ka si ding.

2.Voikhat ninghan riahsiatnak ah, Voikhat Eden ram nuam hmun ah, Daihnak sung le buainak hmun in, Keimah i hruai Pathian kut in.

3.Bawipa na kut kaih ka duh ko, Ziang hmuah ah ka lung a awi ko, Phunzai lo in hnangam te in, Keimah i hruai Pathian kut in.

4.Leitlun ṭuan caan a cem tikah, Na thawm lian in ral ka neh ding, Thihnak ti lian hman ṭih lo in, Bawipa i hruai Jordan kan in.
