185.BAWIPA THAWN FEH KHAT UH

Key-E Forhnak Hla

1.Bawipa thawn feh khat uh, A fehnak kip ah, Na lamzin thim hman seh, A hnen ah ben uh, Ral an hran lai hman ah, Nan thin phang hlah uh, Phannak a um lo ding, Jesuh thawn feh uh.

CHO:   Ṭih lo in, hmai lam ah nor uh, Nan ral ṭhat nak hu suah uh, Runtu Bawi thawn nan feh tlang ding, Vancung tiang in nangmah a hruai ding.

2.Bawipa thawn feh khat uh, Nan fehnak kip ah, Thihnak thim zing hnuai ah, Lamṭha a hmuh ding, Nan lam tleu ter tu cu amah asi ding, Nelrawn sa hman hmundum ah a cang ter ding.

3.Bawipa thawn feh khat uh, Lam lak ah ṭap hlah, A fialmi hmuahhmuah kha ṭha te in ngai uh, A cahnak in nor uh, A kiang ah um uh, Jesuh thawn nan feh ding, Sunnak phang hlah uh.
