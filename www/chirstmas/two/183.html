183.THINGLAMTAH RALKAP KA SI

Key-D Forhnak Hla

1.Thinglamtah ralkap ka si ih, Jesuh dung ka thlun ding, A hnaṭuan kha ka ṭuan ding ih, A hmin ṭha ka sim ding.

CHO:   A hmin sunglawi hmin asi, Ka caan ai thih tu asi, Thinglamtah nak kha phur hman ning, Sui lukhum ka khum ding.

2.Mi hrang laksawng ka ngah ter ding, Ka thih tiang ṭuan ding ah, Vancung thleng ter tu ka si ding, Thihnak ti lian hman seh.

3.Ral do ding ral teh an um maw? Ti lian ṭihnung cung ah, Leitlun ah ral ṭha an um maw? Pathian hnen thleng ding ah.

4.Ral neh ding in kan do tikah, Bawipa in ngam ter aw, Rethei zonzai ka tuar hman ah, Na thu ṭha'n in bawm aw.
