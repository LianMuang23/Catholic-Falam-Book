121.AW BAWIPA HI KANMAIH SUMMI

Key-G Thawinak Hla

1.Aw Bawipa hi kan summi hi thianghlim te in, In rak sang aw, Aw Bawipa Hi kan summi hi in rak sang aw.

CHO:   In rak sang aw, In rak sang aw, Aw Bawipa hi kan sum mi hi in rak sang aw.

2.Aw Bawipa Sabitti Sangreu, Thianghlim te in in rak sang aw, Aw Bawipa Sabitti Sangreu, In rak sang aw.

3.Aw Bawipa kan ruangpumpi khal, Thianghlim te in in rak sang aw, Aw Bawipa kan ruangpumpi khal, In rak sang aw.
