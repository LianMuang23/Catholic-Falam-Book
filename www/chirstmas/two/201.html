201.ALLELUIA JESUH

Key-A Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Vancung mithiang Gabriel, Bawinu Maria thawn tong aw, Nunau hmuahhmuah ih lak ah, Thianghlim cungnungbik na si, Fapa duhnung hring ding ih, Jesuh tin nan ko leh ding, A sim Maria in a rak sang.

CHO:   Alleluia, Amah hmin kan thangṭhat, Amah hmin ka thangṭhat pei, Kumkhua nunnak in pek tu lungawi in ka thangṭhat pei, Jesuh a hmin mawi kan thangṭhat.

2.Hlanlai kan pi kan pu cu, Khuavang kut sung ah an um, An fehnak lamzin tla cu, Kumkhua mit lo Hell mei sung, Fapa Jesuh in tlen hnu, Kumkhua nunnak khi kan ngah, Jesuh a hmin mawi kan thangṭhat.

3.Thinglamtah par an tar ih, Mi hmuahhmuah ih thei dingin, Judah Siangpahrang ti ah, Amah thawm tum liannak cu, A ral misual pawl khal in, Judah Siangpahrang an ti, Jesuh a hmin mawi kan thangṭhat.
