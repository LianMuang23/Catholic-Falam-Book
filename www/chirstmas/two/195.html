195.CHRIST RALKAP PAWL

Key-F Forhnak Hla

1.Christian ralkap hmai ah raldo ding feh uh, Kan hmai ah afeh tu Jesuh khi zoh uh, Kan Bawipa Jesuh Khrih raldo hmai in thi, A thantar mi zoh uh, hmai ah nor uh si.

CHO:   Jesuh ralkap hmai ah raldo ding feh uh, Kan hmai ah afeh tu Jesuh kha zoh uh.

2.Jesuh hmin in Satan ralkap an tlan ih, Christian ralkap in neh, Jesuh bawmnak in thangṭhat nak hla sak uh, U le nau au cio uh nan aw hmuah suah in.

3.Ralkap a feh bangin Christian kawhhran mi Mithiang fehnak hmun ah, Khrih lam thlun uh si, Ṭhen aw mi kan si lo, Pumkhat kan si zo, Zumnak le zaangfah nak hmun khat kan si zo.

4.Leitlun sun lawinak hmuah liam in ral hman seh, Jesuh Khrih kawhhran hmuah kumkhua in hmun seh, Hellram ih neh lo ding, Zumnak hram fek ding, Thu in tiam mi bang in, Kumkhua in hmun ding.
