192.TUAN THLANG UH SI

Key-C Forhnak Hla

1.Duhdawtnak Pathian ram hmu thei ding zuam uh, Lairam Christian mino pawl tla zuam cio uh, Lungawi thu kan sim ding, tha tho lungawi zet in, Leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah feh thlang uh si.

CHO:   {Feh uh, feh uh mi hmuah hnen ah, Lungawinak thu ṭha sim ding, Feh thlang uh si.}2

2.Keimah hmin in satan khal rak dawi suak uh, Nunthar ṭongthar thawn Pathian thu va sim uh, Lungawi thu kan sim ding, Thatho lungawi zet in, Leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah feh thlang uh si.

3.Khuathim sung ih ahlo mi pawl cu hawl uh, Bawipa Jesuh Christian Pawlpi thu sim uh, Lungawi thu kan sim ding, Thatho lungawi zet in, Leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah feh thlang uh si.

4.Zirh uh ka sim bang an thlun theinak dingin, Leitlun cem tiang in umpi ding va sim uh, Lungawi thu kan sim ding, Thatho lungawi zet in, Leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah feh thlang uh si.
