146.PATHIAN DUHDAWTNAK

Key-A Missa Sannak Hla

1.Pathian duhdawtnak cu a tum, Vancung in lei ah a ṭum, Ka lung sung ah nangmah um seh, In zaangfahnak sunglawi seh, Khuaruah thiam mi Bawi Jesuh cu, Duhnak thiannak Bawipa cu, Runnak keng in ka hnen ra aw, Mi thinlung sung rung lut aw.

2.Vansannak neimi lakah hin, na Thiang Thlarau lut ter in, Nangmaih ro kha in run co ter, Na thukam bangin kim ter, A lianbik mi nangmah runtu, Zaangfahnak hmuahhmuah nei tu, Kumkhua in ka hnen ah um seh, Ka hnen in kir nawn hlah seh.

3.Nangmaih hnaṭuan cu a kim thlang, Soisel um lo in a thiang, In runnak thu cu thei cio in, Nangmaih duhnak bangtuk in, Thangṭhat dingin, Vancung khua ah, Na ke hram ka din tikah, Sunlawinak sui lukhuh khum ding, Khuaruah harnak um lo ding.

4.Na ruah cia bangin tuah tharnak, Sualnak in in thian ter aw, Famkim te in na rundamnak, Kim zet in in ngah ter aw, Thawmtumnak ṭhangso ter ringring, Vancungram kan thlen hlan tiang, Lungawi thangṭhat na duhnak sung, Nangmah hnen ka thleng hlan tiang.
