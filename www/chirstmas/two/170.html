170.I HRIAL HLAH

Key-G Dilnak Hla

1.In runtu Bawipa i hrial hlah, Ka kawhnak ngai aw, Mi dang hmuahhmuah na kawh tikah, I run hrial hlah aw.

CHO:   Runtu Bawipa, Ka kawhnak ngai aw, Mi dang hmuahhmuah na kawh tikah, I run hrial hlah aw.

2.Na zaangfahnak tokham kiang ah, Lung daihnak hmu in, Khukbil in thla ka cam tikah, I run bawm hram aw.

3.Na ṭhatnak lawng cu rinsan in, Na hmai hmu ka duh, Thlarau lungsia mi dam ter in, Sualthian nak ka duh.

4.Hnangam ter tu cerhtii na si, Nunnak a sunglawi, Leitlun ah kei hnemtu na si, Vancung ah in cawi.
