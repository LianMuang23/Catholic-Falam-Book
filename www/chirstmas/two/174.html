174.JESUH HRANGAH THO UH

Key-A Forhnak Hla

1.Jesuh hrang ah tu tho uh, Thinglamtah ralkap tla, A than tar sang ah khai uh, Sunnak cang ter hlah uh, Nehnak in a cem net tiang, Ralkap hmuah hruai a duh, Ral hmuahhmuah a neh hlan tiang, Bawibik cu rin san uh.

2.Jesuh hrangah tu tho uh, Mumek aw khi ngai uh, Ṭih lo in hmai va nor uh, Sunlawinak ni asi, Nannih a maih hrang ṭuan uh, Do ding ral a tampi, Nan thazaang kha vasuah uh, Ral ṭhatnak in neh uh.

3.Jesuh hrang ah tu tho uh, Amah khi rinsan uh, Nanmah lawng in do hlah uh, Nan thazaang ring hlah uh, Thuthangṭha ral phaw hruk uh, Thlacamnak thawn hngak uh, Ral do ding aum ah cun, Jesuh thawn in do uh.

4.Jesuh hrangah tu tho uh, Donak a rei lo ding, Tui ni donak aw nan thei, Thaizing neh thawm thei ding, Ral neh mi hmuahhmuah in, Nunnak lukhuh khum uh, Siangpahrang thangṭhatnak in, A kumkhua in uk ding.
