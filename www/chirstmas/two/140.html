140.RINSAN HNGAKHLAPMI JESUH

Key-A Missa Sannak Hla

1.Bawipa Jeush in rawl ei puai a tuah, Kanmah pawl khal in rak ko ringring, A ei tu hmuah in kan tlak lo hrangah, In zaangfah ruangah puai zawh in ko.

CHO:   Rin san Bawipa, Leilung tlun mi nung hmuahhmuah in, Rin san Bawipa, Kanmah pawl hnen ah ṭum thlang aw.

2.Runtupa Jesuh leitlun ah a ṭum, Mifarah harsa kanmah hrangah, Netabik zan ah amaih ruangpumpi, A tuah kanmah ih thlarau hrang ah.

3.Leilungtlun thilri a hmun ringring lo, Thinlung nuamnak khal a kim thei lo, Thu hman thinnuamnak Jesuh hnen a um, Kanmah kumkhua in a pek ringring.

4.Vancung sui khuapi uk tu Siangpahrang, Kanmah in kan thangṭhat thei dingin, Zaangfahnak kim mi in rak pek ringring, Nitin a mah thawn kan um tlang pei.
