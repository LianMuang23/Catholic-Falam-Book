105.SERSIAM MI HMUAH IN

Key-F Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Leitlun mi hmuah in Pathian thangṭhat uh, Sersiam mi hmuah in kumkhua thangṭhat uh, A zaangfahnak tla siar cawk lo asi, Cat lo in Bawipa cu thangṭhat uh si.

CHO:  A kaa in suak mi, Sunglawi ṭongkam cu, Catloin thlun ding in, Kan thinlung sungah um camcin ter aw.

2.Sal tannak in kan luat theinak dingin, Minung thuanthu a hman in a rem ih, Judah miphun tla a hril dah asi, Rundamnak cu kanmah in pek asi.

3.Sal tannak in kan luat caan kim tikah, A Fapa neihsun a run thlah asi, Sual ṭhatlonak in in rundam asi, Kummkhua nunnuamnak cu in pek asi.

4.Thlarau Thianghlim cu a rung ṭum ter ih, Dungthluntu tla catlo a kilkhawi ih, A sunglawi ṭongkam cu in pek asi, Kumkhua laksawng kanmah in pek asi.
