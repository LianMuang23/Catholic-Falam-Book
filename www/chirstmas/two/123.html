123.CALVARY TLANGAH

Key-C Thawinak Hla
CHO:   Calvary tlang par ah, Bawipa Jesuh thawinak cu, Na rak sang bangtukin, Kan sumnak khal in sang aw.

1.Tlangbawipa in sang le sabitti, Pa Pathian hnenah a sum ih, Tlangbawipa thinlung khat te in, Pa Pathian hnen ah kan sum pei.

2.Duhdawtnak thawn in, Pa Pathian kan lo biak asi, A san tlai lo mi kanmaih sumank cu, Jesuh hmai zah in in rak sang aw.

3.Tlangbawipa thawn kom tlang in, Leilung tlun na thu thlun tu pawl in, Thinlung hmun khat te in, Pa Pathian cu kan biak cio pei.
