106.PATHIAN INN ṬHA

Key-A Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Tlang par ih thingram lak ah biakinn aum, Hi hmun hnak ṭha deuh a um lo, Khi hmun Pathian inn ṭha bangtukin, Hmun dang mawi deuh ka hrang aum lo.

CHO:   Pathian biakinn mawi ah ra uh, Hla in Bawipa thangṭhat uh si, Amaih inn nuam kan thlengnak ding ah, Ni tin ten a thu kham thlun ding ah.

2.Ziangtluk a nuam Bawipa nitlann zawngah khin, Khawnglawng tum thawng kan thei tikah, Pathian hnen ah in rak ko rero, Ra cio uh law Bawipa biak uh si.

3.Arsi tlun ah Pathian khuaruahhar inn cu, Vanmi khawnglawng tumnak thawng khi, Kan hmuh le kan theih ringring tikah, Ziangtluk in so kan rak nuam leh ding.
