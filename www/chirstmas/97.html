97.ZARHPI NI UL UH

Key-F Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Siangpahrang hmuah lakah a tum bik Bawi, A thu kam bang in Lungawinak thawn, Ai puannak le lungawinak thinlung thawn, Zarhpi ni cu thianghlim ter uh.

CHO:   Zumnak nei mi hmuahhmuah ra cio uh, Thianghlim mi Biakinn ah, Pathian cu rual rem ten kan biak pei, Biakinn thianghlim ah ra cio uh.

2.A sim cia mi thu zumnak neih nak le, Thinlung duhnak in Upatnak thawn, Amah bawmnak cu amah kan dil pei, Banglo in hla kan sak tlang pei.

3.Zarhpi ni cu upatnak in thian ter, Ni hmuahhmuah lak ah ni thiang bik, Pathian thangṭhatnak hla khi kan sak pei, Dai zir ziar in kar ul cio pei.

4.Zaangfahnak thawn a kim mi kan Bawipa, Upat ding a tlak zarhpi ni cu, Tha te'n ul theinak ding in run bawm aw, Lawmnak hla sak Alleluia.
