43.JESUH LAWNGIN A PHUR DING MAW?

Key-A Vua Hla

1.Jesuh lawng in a phur ding maw? Thinglamtah phur ve aw, Mi hmuahhmuah phur ding aum ve, Ka hrang ah a um ve.

2.Khi thinglamtah phur ka ngam ko, Thihnak in a neh lo, Van inn nuam ah sui lukhuh khum, Ka hrang khum ding aum.

3.An khen mi Jesuh kehram ah, A tleumi lung sungah, Ka sui lukhuh cu ka khum ding, A hmin ka thangthat ding.

4.Khi thinglamtah a sunglawi mi, A hmin thang thawhsal Ni, Vancung khua in vanmi tum uh, Keimai thlarau lak uh.
