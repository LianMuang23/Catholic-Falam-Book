36.THINGLAMTAH KIANGAH

Key-F Vua Hla

1.Calvary thinglamtah ah, Damnak thi a luang zo, Mi hmuah thian ternak dingah, Calvary in luang zo.

CHO:   Thinglamtah, thinglamtah, Kan uar bik mi asi, Vancung khua kan thleng tikah, Kan nuamnak ding asi.

2.Thinglamtah kiang ka dingih, Zaangfahnak thawn i hmu, Deirel Arsi a tleu ih, Ka kimvel tleu ka hmu.

3.Jesuh thinglamtah kiangah, Hngak in i um ter aw, Thinglamtah phen thlam hnuai ah, Nitin i um ter aw.

4.Ka ding thinglamtah kiangah, Kumkhua in ka zum ding, Tiva ral ka thlen tik ah, Sui khua ah ka um ding.
