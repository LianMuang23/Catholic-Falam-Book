49.JESUH IN KEI I DUH

Key-G Vua Hla

1.Jesuh Bawi Pathian in, kei i duh kumkhua in, Zianghrang ah i duh ka thei lo, Zaangfahnak tumbik thawn a rung tum ih i duh, Ka lung a awi hngin sim thei lo in.

CHO:   Thinglamtah parah Bawi Jesuh ih thi a luang, A tam mi ka sual thiang ter dingah, Bawipa Pathian lawng kei, Kumkhua in ka duh ding, Ka nunnak caan le ni cem tiang in.

2.A rit mi ka sualnak, thiang ternak ding hrangah, Ka hrang ah Bawipa in a phur, A lu hlo tuar tiang in, Bawipa in kei i duh, Amaih velneihnak kei in ka co.

3.Bawipa velneihnak cu, Zum in a hrang hnatuan, Lungawinak hla sa in, thangthat Nunnak sui lukhuh cu, ngah thei nak ding hrangah, Fel le fek zet in hna tuan tlang uh.
