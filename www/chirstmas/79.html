79.JESUH THIANGHLIM THINLUNG

Key-C Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.Aw zaangfahnak tum bik thawn kim tu Jesuh, Kanmah tla kan sualnak a tam na in, Thianghlim thinlung na zaangfahnak inn ong In zaangfahnak laksawng tla in pek hram aw.

CHO:  Aw Jesuh thinlung kanmah pan hmun, Kanmah kan thinlung tla khi, Nangmah hnen kan lo pek, Kanmah kan thinlung tla khi, Nangmah hnen kan lo pek.

2.Na kut sung ih kan ngah mi laksawng tla khi, Kanmah pawl in a tam pi'in kan thei, Na thianghlim pumpi na tuah mi ṭhatnak khi, Ziang tin in so kan lo rul sal thei ding.

3.Bawipa nangmaih zaangfahnak rul duh hrangah, Ka pum le thlarau hmuahhmuah ka lo pek, Keimah hlei ah ka neih mi thil hmuahhmuah khal,Thianghlim Thinlung hnen ah ka lo pek ṭheh.

4.Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung ka lo duhnak khi, Nikhat hnu nikhat a thang sinnak ding, Kanmah pawl in cat bang lo in kan zuam ding, Ti'in tuih sun na hmai ah thu kan kam.
