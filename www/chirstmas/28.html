28.RUNTU BAWI CALVARY AH

Key-F Vua Hla

1.Runtu Bawi Calvary tlang ah, Zonzaih tuar in aum, Nang le kei in duhdawt ruangah, Thinglamtah ah an khen.

CHO:   In duhdawtnak thir khennak in, Thinglamtah ah an khen, Cem theilo mi in duhdawtnak, Nang le kei hrangah maw.

2.Bawipa zonzaih thihnak thawng in, Kan sual man a kuan zo, Pathian Tuufa zonzaihnak in, Kan sualnak a feh pi.

3.Pathian Tuufa thiang khi zoh uh, Na sualnak a feh pi, A thi cu na hrang a thlet zo, Amah kha rinsan uh.
