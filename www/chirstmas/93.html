93.BAWIPA RALNEHNAK

Key-B Jesuh Siangpahrang Hla

1.Kan Bawipa Jesuh hmin thianghlim cu, Leitlun mi'n an thei caan a cu, Leitlun ah Bawipa ram a thleng ding, A thlen theinak ding kan dil ding.

CHO:   Hminthang Siangpahrang hnen ah, Thangṭhatnak um leh law, Duhnak thawn a fa te pawl, Kumkhua in kilkhawi aw.

2.Pathian Fapa kan Siangpahrang in, Leitlun mi hmuahhmuah run dingin, Niam zet in a rung suak zo asi, Runtu Bawipa Jesuh asi.

3.Leitlun mi hrang thinglamtah par ah, Sualnak nei mi pawl run dingah, Siangpahrang Bawi a thi zo asi, Leitlun mi hmuah in run asi.

4.Hmin thang Siangpahrang Bawi Jesuh in, Thawmtum Pathian asi nak in, Catholic Pawlpi a phuan asi, A hmun ih a thang sin asi.
