1.RA AW, IN RUNTU BAWIPA

Key-G Adventu Hla

1.Ra aw in Runtu Bawipa, Leitlun mi in kan hngak hlap tuk; Sual nak sung in runtu ding, Kan lo rinsan asi.

CHO:   Runtu Bawipa zamrang ra aw, Zaangfahnak nei in bawm dingin, Khuathimnak cu rak tleu ter aw, Runtu Bawipa zamrang aw, Leitlun mi in kan hngakhlap tuk, Sualnak hmuah cem ter dingin, Kan lo rinsan asi

2.Naute caw rawl kuang sungah, Lungnemnak thawn a rat tikah, Kan biak ding kan mai Bawipai, Zamrang in hmu ter aw.

3.Prophet pawl sim cia bangin, Leitlun mi in an hngakhlap tuk, Zonzai pawl khal run dingin, Zamrang in rung tum aw.
