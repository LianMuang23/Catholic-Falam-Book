70.VANCUNG AH JESUH A KAI

Key-A Thosal Hla

1.Vancungram ah Jesuh a kai zo, Ralnehnak hla sak in au seh, Meiro lak in Bawipa a kai zo, Vancungmi in an rak hmuak theh. Amah kainak vancung lampi tlun ah, Thawmtum khua tleu phundang asi, Meiro par ah a to, Tui sun ah, (Vancung ah a kai zo asi.)2

2.Kanmah ruangpum ah cuih thu thuk in, A rak khaisang zo a Pawlpi, A dungthlun tu pawl tanta lo in, A fimnak thawn in kil asi. Tongkam in thu in zirh a nun lai ah, Nuncan ding daan in hmuh theh asi, Amaih umnak cuih ramnuam pi ah, (Kan hrang umnak a tuah theh zo)2

3.Bawipa Jesuh in a sim bangin, Ka keneh run thlun uh ati, Jesuh keneh kan thlun tikah hin, Jesuh bang kan nuam ding asi, Runtu Bawipa ih thu kan thlun lawng ah, Vanram nuam co tu kan si ding, Jesuh a kainak hmun vancung ah, (Ka thei zo kei khal ka kai ding)2
