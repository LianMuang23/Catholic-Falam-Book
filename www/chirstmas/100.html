100.BAWIPA HNEN NAN LUNGAWI UH

Key-A Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Thinlung a thiang mi hmuahhmuah in, Bawipa hnen ah lungawi cio uh, Bawi Jesuh a hril mi hmuah tla, Riatsiat thinphannak hlon ṭheh uh.

CHO:   Lawm uh, lawm uh, Bawipa hnen ah lungawi cio uh, Lawm uh, lawm uh, Bawipa hnen ah lungawi cio uh.

2.Bawi Jesuh kha lawm cio uh si, Van le leilung tla khal a uk, A kut sung um mi kan si ih, A nunnak in in tlen ṭheh zo.

3.Bawipa ih ralkap hmuahhmuah tla, Rul hreng in neh zik hman seh la, Jesuh a zum mi hmuahhmuah cu, Ral hmuahhmuah hnak in an cak sawn.

4.Sun caan ah khua thim hman seh la, Khuathim in tleunak phen hman seh, Na thlarau ṭih ding a um lo, Na nunnak cem tiang in zum aw.
