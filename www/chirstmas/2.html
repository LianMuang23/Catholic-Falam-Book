2.RA AW, RA AW, IMMANUEL

Key-G Adventu Hla

1.Ra aw, ra aw, Immanuel, Run tlen aw thawngtla Israel, Riahsiat ninghang an tah lai ah, Pathian Fapa rat glan tiang in

CHO:   Nan lungawi uh, Israel mi pawl, A ra sal ding Immanuel.

2.Ra aw, ra aw, nang Jesse hrin, Satan kut sungin luat ter aw, Hell sung in na miphun run aw, Thihnak ral nehnak run pe aw.

3.Tleunak Bawi zamrang ten ra aw, Na thawm in in lungawi ter aw, Thimnak khua hi run tleu ter in, Tleunak in pe thimnak sungin.

4.Ra aw, David Fa kan Bawipa, In on sak aw vancung sangkaa, Vancungram ah in kilkhawi aw, Nangmah lam ah in rak hruai aw.
