75.PATHIAN LAKSAWNG

Key-F Thiangthlarau Pathian Hla

1.Leitlun ah Pathian ih bomnak lak ah, A sangbik ah minung tla in tuah ih, Ruang pum le thlarau ni tin kan thangnak, Pathian pek mi laksawng asi.

2.Thlarau nunnak lam tha in ruat tertu, A sunglawi bik mi kan thinlung in tuah, Ruahnak tha petu zumnak kan neih nak, Pathian pek mi laksawng asi.

3.Thinglamtah hmai ah in pan duh ter tu, Lam in hruai ih lam tha in hmu ter tu, Khua in hmuh ter tu, tleunak zaangfahnak, Pathian pek mi laksawng asi.

4.Ka thinlung khat in lawmnak thu tha khi, Bawi Jesuh kawh nak aw khi kan thlun pei, Amah hrangah cat lo hna tuan dingin, Pathian laksawng thang hram seh law.

5.Zumnak, rinnak, duhnak Pathian zaangfah, Kan ngah hnu ah khoh ter ringring uh si, Nitin te in kan pumpi nunnak khal, Pathian pek mi laksawng asi.
