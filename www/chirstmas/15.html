15.KAN LAWM TLANG PEI

Key-F Christmas Hla

David umnak khuapi hmunnuam zet, Cuih hmunah caw inn sia te aum, A sung ah Van Bawi Fa a rung suak, Mifim pawl khal in amah an biak.

CHO :   {Thangthat uh, Thangthat uh, Van Bawi Fa a suak zo, Lawm cio uh, A thangthatnak hla in leitlun hmuah thang ko seh, Vancung tiang in thang seh.}2

2.Bethlehem ram ah Phulkolh aum, Tuukhal mi nauta te pawl an um, An hnen ah Pathian ih Vancung mi'n, Lungawinak Bawi thar suah thu an sim.

3.Sak lam mifim pawl in an thei ih, Arsi hminthang zet hngin thlun phah in, Cawrawl kuang ih Bawi suak an hmuh in, Lungawi zet in amah cu an biak

4.Cuih arsi kan hrang ah tleu ve aw, Hlan ih mifim pawl na hruai bangin, Bethlehem khua ah in hruai ve aw, Bawi thar suak mi cu kan biak ve ding.
