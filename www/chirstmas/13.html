13.JUDEA RAM BETHLEHEM KHUA KIANG

Key-F Christmas Hla

1.Judea ram Bethlehem khua kiang, Fala thianghlim Maria in, Minung hmuahhmuah in runtu dingah, A Naute khi suak zo asi.

CHO :   Alleluia, lungawi khat in, Alleluia leilung tlun ah, Pathian Bawipa suak zo asi.

2.Tuukhal pawl in hlat lo hmun ihsin, Tuu fate tla an kil lai ah, Vancungmi in fiang zet langnak in, Thawmtumnak khi a thleu asi.

3.Tuukhal pawl in a thawm an hmuh ah, An thinphang in an khur lai ah, Anmah hnenah vancungmi pawl in, Tih hlah uh law, thawm rak lawm uh.

4.Minung hmuahhmuah in duhnak tum bik, Lungawinak thu than ding kan thleng, Minung hmuahhmuah in runtu dingah, Christu Bawipa tuihsun a suak.

5.David khua kiang cawrawl kuang sungah, Thawmtum Naute a suak asi, Fang kuangtlun ah puante'n an tuam mi, Van le lei Bawi it in a um.
