83.JESUH THINLUNG HNENAH

Key-D Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.Jesuh Bawipa Thianghlim Thinglung, In duhdawtnak tum zet a nei, Duh zet nak thawn Jesuh Thinlung, Kan thangṭhat pei, Kan thangṭhat pei.

CHO:   Jesuh thinlung hnen ah, Duhnak le upatnak um ringring seh, Um ringring seh.

2.Ruat cio hnik uh in duhdawtnak, Jesuh Bawipa hnen ah dil uh, A tu thok in Bawipa'i duhnak, Rul sal dingin rak zuam cio uh.

3.Jesuh Bawipa kan thlun tikah, Kan duhnak khi a kim ṭheh ding, Kanmah Bawipa thinlung hnen ah, Lungawinak thawn in kan neih ding.

4.Nem zet nak le thinniamnak thawn, A khat ṭheh aw Jesuh Bawipa, Kan thinlung cu na thinlung thawn, Bangrep ding in tuah aw Bawipa.
