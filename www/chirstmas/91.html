91.JESUH HMIN THANGTHAT UH

Jesuh Siangpahrang Hla

1.Bawipa Jesuh hmin thangṭhat uh, Vanmi in thangṭhat uh, Vanmi in thangṭhat seh, Sunglawi lukhuh khum ter si seh.

CHO:   Bawibik, Bawibik, Bawibik, Bawibik, Bawibik, Bawipa'n khum seh Bawipa, Bawipa'n khum seh.

2.A hril mi Judah phun nan si, (A taang mi mal nan si)2 Runtu Bawipa khi sunglawi seh.

3.Miphun kip le hrin za hmuah in, (Vanrang hnuai hmuahhmuah in)2 Sunlawinak amai ta si seh.

4.Zential mi pawl nan sual hman ah, (In runnak thu ngilh hlah)2 Hminthannak a ke ah ṭhum seh.

5.Hi leitlun ih miphun kip in, (Jesuh lian bik ti in)2 Liannak hmuah amai ta siseh.

6.Amai mithianghlim pawl thawn in, (A ke hram ah bok in)2 Bawi Jesuh cu thangṭhat siseh.
