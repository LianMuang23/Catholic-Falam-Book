64.IN TLENTU CU THANGTHAT UH

Key-G Thosal Hla

1.Tho sal mi Bawi cu lawm uh, Alleluia, Duhdawt ruang in in tlen sal zo, Rundamnak sangkaa asi, Alleluia, Mi hmuah thu rel ding in a tho.

CHO:   A duhdawtnak ka sim thiam lo, In tlen tu cu thangthat cio uh, Leitlun hmuah hi lawm uh au in, Alleluia ti in sak seh.

2.Mero cu a kiang ih thimnak cu a hlo, Duhdawt hmel ih a lang awk nak, Nunau thianghlim pawl in amah cu hmu in, Bawipa a thosal zo an ti.

3.Leitlun mi in amah hmuhsuam hman sehla, Thawmtum zet in a thosal zo, Thlarau riahsiat hrang zangfahnak hrampi cu, Kumkhua kan lungawinak asi.
