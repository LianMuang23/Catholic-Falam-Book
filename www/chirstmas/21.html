21.NA UMNAK TAANIN

Key-G Christmas Hla

1.Vancung sunlawinak sui lukhum tan in, Leitlun ah na rung tum tikah, Sihmanseh Bethlehem na thleng ah hin, Umnak hman nei lo cuih khua ah.

CHO:   Bawi Jesuh ka lung sung rung tum, Na hrangah umnak khaan aum, Bawipa Jesuh ka lung sung rung tum, Na hrangah umnak khaan aum.

2.Vancungmi'n lungawi in hla an sak mi cu, Na sunlawinak hla tha asi, Sihmanseh Naute bangin Bawipa cu, Thinnem ngaingai mi Bawi asi.

3.Cinghnia in kua an nei vate in bu an nei, Tu thingsang umnak daihnem ah, Pathian Fa ramthing a hlum ti ka thei, Galilee ram vunnel rawn ah.

4.Ka Bawipa nunnak thu thawn na ra ih, Ka sual hmuahhmuah in thlen asi, Hmuhsuamnak hling lukhuh cu an khum ih, Calvary ah khen in na thi.
