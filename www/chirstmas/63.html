63.JESUH THIHNAK A NEH ZO

Key-G Thosal Hla

1.Jesuh thihnak a neh zo, A huham thawn lian in thlankua sungin a tho sal, Jesuh thihnak a neh zo, Minung hrin phunkim in a run zo, Kanmah mawhnak le kanmah sualnak a tam naan, Bawipa Jesuh in kanmah tla in zaangfah ih, Sual nei tu tla kan sualnak hmuahhmuah khi, A tu thok in sup kan zuam hram pei.

2.Thu thlun tu u le nau tla, Tui sun Bawipa Jesuh, Khuavang tla khi a Neh zo, Thuthluntu u le nau tla, Aw khat in lawmnak hla kan sak pei, Vancung Siangpahrang Bawi thinglamtah par, Thihnak khi tuar in kanmah tla in rak run zo, Jesuh ralnehnak Alan thinglamtah, Lungawi aipuang zet in kan thlun pei.

3.Jesuh cu a nung sal zo, minung za te tla khi, Netabik ni nung ding in, Jesuh cu a nung sal zo, Khuavang bawi ram khi a siat theh zo, Kanmah runtu Pa Bawipa Jesuh in, Satan a neh zo hrangah, tihnak nei hlah uh, Jesuh in duhnak khi, Kan ruat pei ih, Kan thin lung za te thawn kan duh pei.
