81.PATHIAN DUHNAK MEI IN

Key- Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.Pathian duhnak mei in a kang theinak ding, Vancungram in leitlun ah ka rung ṭum, Cuih duhnak mei in mi hmuah thinlung sungah, A kang lo le khaw ka lung a kim lo.

CHO:   A upa bik Jesuh thianghlim thinlung khi, Keimah kumkhua duhnak thawn in ka duh, Keimah ka thinlung duhnak mei bang kangnak thawn, Bawipa ka duh ringring ding asi.

2.Ka thinlung in nangmah ka duhnak thu khi, Ka duh fapa na tahṭhim thei lo ding, Na thei le lungawinak thawn ka hnen ra in, Na pum zate keimah hnen na pek ding.

3.Feipa thawn dawtnak tuar ka thinlung sungah, Zumnak nei hrih lo mi pawl in zum uh, Aw sual nei mi pawl sir uh la thiang ter uh, Aw miding pawl dai thlang lo zuam sin uh.
