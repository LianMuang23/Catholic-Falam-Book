54.A LIAN BIK TUUFA

Key-D Thosal Hla

1.A lian bik mi Pathian Tuufa, lawm cio uh si, A hmun mi Bawi a tho sal zo, Lawm cio uh si, Kan hrang kumkhua nunnuamnak cu.

CHO:   Lungawi in thangthat cio uh si, Thihnak neh tu asi hrang ah, In run zo, i duhdawt ruangah, Tuufa te cu kan thangthat ding.

2.Thlan kua sung in a tho sal zo, Alleluia in runtu Bawi, A lianbik duhnak sunglawi bik, Nunnak cirhti kha a rung cang.

3.Puan var sin in lennak ram ah, Van mi thawn hmun khat ah leng in, Remnak Bawipa cu thangthat in, A tho sal zo, Alleluia.
