67.SUNLAWI LUKHUH

Key-G Thosal Hla

1.Leitlun mi hmuhhmuah in zoh uh, Riatsiatnak a tuar Jesuh, A ral hmuahhmuah a neh theh zo, Mi hmuah in an thangthat.

CHO: Sunglawi lukhuh, sunglawi lukhuh, Bawi Jesuh kha khum ter uh, A lian bik mi Bawi Jesuh cu, Siangpahrang, Siangpahrang.

2.Vancung khua um mi hmuahhmuah in, Bawi Jesuh an thangthat cio, Thu relnak tohkheng an to ter, Vancung mi in an lawm.

3.Hling lukhuh kha misual pawl in, Bawi Jesuh cu an khum ter, Mithiang mi le vancung mi in, Jesuh hmin kha an thangthat.

4.Amah lawm in au uh, Ralnehnak puai an tuah, Sunlawinak tumpi a hmu zo, Zoh uh Jesuh.
