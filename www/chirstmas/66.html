66.ZOH AW, VAN BAWIFA

Key-A Thosal Hla

1.Zoh aw van Bawi fa an tarnak hmun cu, Leitlun Bawi Van Bawi in a neh zo, An khar mi lungpi cu a ong zo, Kan that sual van Bawi Fapa aw an to.

CHO:   An tar mi thangthat uh, Pathian Fapa a tho zo, Profet pawl sim bang in, Nang le kei sualnak thian ter duh ah, Thihnak in a tho, Van Bawi Fapa cu.

2.A zumtu Mary Magdalena le, A thluntu hrekkhat pawl cu an feh, Zingpit ten thlan khar lungpi kiang lam ah, A tho zo tin van mi in a sim.

3.A dungthlun pawl hnen a lang sal tikah, Nan hnen ah daihnak um seh a ti, Leitlun cem tiang in nan hnennah ka um ding, Feh uh la, Thu Thangtha va sim uh.
