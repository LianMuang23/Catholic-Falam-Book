65.LEILUNG THAWH TIRAH

Key-D Thosal Hla

1.Leilung a thawtir ah khua thim sung bang, Leitlun misual tlen tu thinglamtah par, A thi mi van Bawi Fa, Thawm liannak thawn a tho.

CHO:   A tho, a tho, Christu Thawmliannak thawn a tho sal zo, Vanmi, thlan khar, a ong, Thawmliannak thawn a tho sal zo.

2.Mi thianghlim Joseph in a ruak a lak, Nithum hnu ah thihnak in a tho sal, Vancungram sangkaa cu, Mi hmuahhmuah hrang a ong.

3.Vancung uktu a Pa hnen ah a kai, Pathian vorhlam sui tohkheng tlun a to, Leilung cem tik misual, Mi ding thuthen ding ah.

4.Thlarau thu rel tu le nunnak uk tu, Siangpahrang lak ah Siangpahrang tum bik, Aw Bawipa in bawm aw, Leitlun ka nun sung ah.
