82.JESUH THIANGHLIM THINLUNG HNENAH

Key-G Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.Leitlun mi pawl duh tu bik, Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung, Aw remnak thawn thlun nak in, Lu kun in kan biak pei.

CHO:   A nem zet le nun ṭha mi, Jesuh thianghlim thinlung, Upatnak le biaknak khi, (Baang lo in um seh law)2

2.Zaangfah duhnak baang thei lo, Duhṭulnak baang um lo, Jesuh thianghlim thinlung khi, Kumkhua in kan lawm pei.

`

3.Nemnak le supnak kim tu, Aw Bawi Jesuh Pathian, Na thinlung le kei thinlung, Khat thei ding in tuah aw.
