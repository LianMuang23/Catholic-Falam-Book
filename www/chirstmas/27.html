27.LEITLUN AUM HLANAH

Key-F Vua Hla

1.Leilung aum hlan ah ruah cia bangin, Thu thlam a um cia zo, Ziang hmuahhmuah cu amah in a tuah, Cemnak nei lo Pathian asi.

CHO:   A thukham bangtukin a rak um zo, Leitlun ah kanmaih Bawipa cu leitlun zonzaihnak phunkim tla thawn, A thukham kim ter ding hrang ah, Thihnak naa bik a tuar.

2.Amah duhnak leilung tlun ah, Sun ni tleu bangin a tleu ding, Sualnak hmuahhmuah a rak neh thei tu, Bawipa hmin cu Jesuh asi.

3.Hi leitlun thimnak ram sungah, A mawinak le vel neihnak cu, A thawmtumnak leilung hninnak thawn, Thinglamtah nak Jesuh'n a tuar.

4.Thinglamtah par an tar lai ah, Bawmtu zo tal an um pei maw? Kanmah misual in ruat tuk hrangah, Thisen luangin thihnak a tuar.
