57.BAWIPA THAWHSAL CAAN

Key-G Thosal Hla

1.A thosal zo Alleluia, Jesuh vancung a kai zo, Thihnak sangkaa cu a ong zo, Vanmi aw thawng khal ka thei, (A tho sal zo, a tho sal zo, Thihnak tla cu a neh zo)2

2.A tho sal zo Alleluia, Runtu Bawi tumbik asi, Hnangam tu Thiangthlarau a thlah, Kanmah bawmnak pek dingin, (A tho sal zo, a tho sal zo, A hnen in dingnak kan hmu)2

3.A tho sal zo Alleluia, Thihnak cu a neh theh zo, Jesuh cu thawhsalnak asi, Thlan sung in a thosal zo, (A tho sala zo, a tho sal zo, Siangpahrang Bawipa asi)2
