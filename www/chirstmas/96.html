96.MIPHUN HMUAHHMUAH

Key-F Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Miphun hmuahhmuah Pathian hnen ah, Lungawinak tum zet thawn lawm uh, Pathian duhnak thukhamnak khi, (Tha nuamnak thawn in thlun uh law.)2

2.Kumkhua ih um Pathian lawng in, Kan Bawipa kan Pathian asi, Thawmtum Bawipa Pathian hnen ah, (Lungawinak thawn in hla sak uh)2

3.Bawipa Pathian in kan zate'n, A tuah ṭheh ih in kil ringring, Kanmaih Bawipa Pathian hnen ah, (Lungawinak thawn in hla sak uh)2

4.Mi hmuahhmuah in Pathian minung, Lungawinak thawn in hung khawm uh, Biakinn sungin Pathian tlun ah, (Aw khat tlang in hla sak uh law)2
