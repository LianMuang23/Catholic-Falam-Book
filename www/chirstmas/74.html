74.THIANG THLARAU PATHIAN

Key-F Thiangthlarau Pathian Hla

1.A tumbik Thiang Thlarau Pathian, Ni dang lai ih dungthlun pawl tlun, Tha thar pek ding na tum bang in, Tuih sun khal rung tum aw.

2.Kanmah kan thinlung tla sungah, Thianghlim zaangfahnak in pe aw, Zum theinak ding zaangfahnak khal, Thiang Thlarau rak pe aw.

3.Thinlung thatlonak pawl hmuah tla, Kanmah tla in rak thian ter aw, Mi thianghlim kan suak theinak ding, Zaangfahnak thang ter aw.

4.Pathian Bawipa thu le hla khi, A thei fiang lo mi pawl tlun ah, A tum bik Thiang Thlarau Pathian, Khua tleu nak rak pe aw.

5.Pa Pathian le Fapa Pathian, A bang rep ih kan biak bangin, Thiang Thlarau Pathian tlun khal ah, Lawmnak khi um seh law.
