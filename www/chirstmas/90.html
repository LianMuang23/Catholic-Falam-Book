90.VANCUNG KAN PA (2)

Key-G Thumkom Pathian Hla

1.Lei le van a sersiam tu Pa, Lei le va Bawi asi, Runnak pek in zaangfah thiam Pa, Na hmin kan lawm asi, Nangmah a thangṭhat duhtu pawl, Van mi'n an dawn thei lo, Kan thinlung le kan aw neih pawl, Nangmah hnen kan ap zo.

2.Pathian Fapa Jesuh Khrih in, Thlennak a tuah asi, Leitlun misual pawl nung dingin, Rundamnak zin asi, Tuu in suak mi Christu Bawipa, Upatnak thawn kan lawm, Mi hmuah in Runtu kan Bawipa, Kan lo hmuak kan lo lawm.

3.Thiang Thlarau Pathian thawmtumbik, Ṭhatnak hmuahhmuah hrampi, Leilung tlun ih khuathimnak bik, Van zin na hmuh asi, Bawmtu Pathian pakhat lawm in, Lawmnak hla sak uh si, Thawmtumnak hminṭhatnak hmuah in nangmah na ta asi.
