8.BAWI KHRIH SUAHNI AHCUN

Key-G Christmas Hla

1.Bawi Jesuh Khrih suahni ah cun, Lei le vancung a hnin in, Vanmi pawl in hla in an lawm, Mithianghlim le Seraphim thawn.

CHO :   Vansangbik ah thawmtum seh law, ti in Vanmi inn an thangthat, Leitlun ah thinlung tha hnenah, Remnak thleng seh ti ah an sak

2.An hla mawi ih thangkhawk cu, Tuukhal pawl in an rak thei ih, David ih khua Bethlehem ah, Kan hrang Naute suah thu an sim.

3.Tlunlam Arsi tleu mawi zet cun, A thawmtumnak a lang ter ih, Naute thianghlim in Runtu cu, Leilung in thangthat hla sak seh.

4.Mifim pawl in upat zet in, Kun in cibai an run buk ih, Sui le hlawban le rimhmui pawl, Duhsan nei mi laksawng an pek.
