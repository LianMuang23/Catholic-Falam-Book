89.ZAANGFAHNAK PHUNDANG

Key-E Thumkom Pathian Hla

1.Zaangfahnak phunphun pek tu pa, Bawipa lawng kan thangṭhat ding, A kumkhua in thawmtum bik Pa, Cat lo in kan thangṭhat ding.

CHO:   Pa, Fapa le Thiang Thlarau, Lei mi in kan thangṭhat ding, Bawi thum Pathian ṭhen aw thei lo, Cat lo in kan thangṭhat ding.

2.A thawmtumnak tah ṭhim thei lo, Van mi pawl Bawipa lawm uh, Bawipa lawmnak a cem thei lo, Cat lo in thang ṭhat cio uh.
