6.BAWIPA RA TIKAH RALRINNAK DING

Key-A Adventu Hla

1.Bawipa rat caan a nai ralring ten um cio uh, Thuthlun u le naus pawl tha te in ruat uh, Bawipa luhnak ding kan thinlung rem tha uh a caan a um lai ah hin

CHO :   Bawipa in, in ko zo, Ralring cia uh in ti, Nang le kei in duh ruangah, Reitheinak phunphun a tuar asi, Lungawi rul sal lo in na um thei ding maw?

2.Hni mawi thil mawi kedem mawca thawn si lo in, Thinlung thianghlim thawn duhhamnak pawl hlawn in, In kawhnak thu tha khoh zetin kan sang ding, Thinniamnak thawn in kan timtuah sawn ding.

3.Rawl ei ti in nunnuamnak pawl hamaisa lo in, Thinlung thianghlim thawn duhhamnak pawl hlawn in, Lam in hmuhnak pawl ruat in ka thlun sawn ding, Bawipa'n in bawm ding kan thawm a tleu ding.
