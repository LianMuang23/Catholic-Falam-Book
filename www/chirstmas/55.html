55.AW FAPA LE FA NU PAWL

Key-C Thosal Hla

1.Aw fapa le fanu pawl tla, Tumbik vancung Siangpahrang Bawi, Tuih sun a nung sal zo asi, Alleluia.

CHO:   Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia

2.Maria Magdalena le Jacob ih nu Salome in, Hriak rim tha thawn thlan ah an feh, Alleluia.

3.Magdalena a sim bang in, Jesuh phumnak thlan an thleng tiang in, Dungthlun pahnih zamrang an tlan, Alleluia.

4.Sikhalsehla dungthlun John cu, Peter, hmai ah zamrang tlan ih, Thlankua hmun ah a thleng hmaisa, Alleluia.

5.Vancungmi in puanrang sin in, Nunau pawl hnen thu thannak khi, Bawi Jesih khi a nung sal zo, Alleluia.

6.Dungthlun tu pawl um khawm lai ah, An lai fang ah Jesuh ding in, Nanmah nan thin nuamnak um seh, Alleluia.
