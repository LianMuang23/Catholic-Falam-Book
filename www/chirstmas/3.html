3.NGI UH, THAWNGVANG KA THEI

Key-C Adventu Hla

1.Ngai uh, thawngvang ka thei mi cu, Lam sialtu aw asi, Jordan tiva ralkhat lam tiang, Lam sialtu John asi.

2.Vancung uktu a ra cing ding, Thinlung thiang in hngak uh, Runtu Messiah a rat tikah, Tleunak thawn a ra ding.

3.Runtu Messiah a rat tikah, Laksawng then ding hmuahhmuah, Thinlung thiang te in ralring uh, A rat hlan tiang hngak uh.

4.Sualnak hmuahhmuahsir aw uhla, Nan lung sangka ong uh, Prophet John a sim mi thu cu, Lung thlengnak aw asi.
