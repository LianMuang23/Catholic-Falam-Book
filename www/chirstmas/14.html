14.NOEL VANCUNGMI HLA

Key-D Christmas Hla

1.Noel vancungmi hla saknak, Lo lam farah tuu khal hnenah, Lo lam um mi tuu khal lai ah, Khuasik tikcuu zantiim lai ah.

CHO :   Noel, Noel, Noel, Noel, Isreal Siangpahrang a suak.

2.Tlunlam zoh in a hlat pi ah, (Nisuahnak lam Arsi tleu an hmu)2, Sum zan catlo banglo a tleu.

3.Arsi nitlak lam a pil zawngah, Bethlehem khua tlunah a cawl, Cuih Arsi colhnak ah an ding, Bawi Jesuh suahnak hmun tlun ah.

4.Cuih mi Arsi tleu thlun phah in, Siangpahrang Bawipa hawl dingin, Mifim pathum ram hlat an thleng, Arsi hruainak lam ah an feh.

5.Mifim pathum cuih hmun an lut, Upatnakthawn khukbil phah in, Sui le rimhmui le zihmui cu, Bawipa hnenah laksawng an pek.
