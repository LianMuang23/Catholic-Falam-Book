MAW A LIAN BIK

Key-D Christmas Hla

1.Maw a lian bik Alleluia, Kan lo thangthat Emmanuel, Sunglawinak le uknak hmuahhmuah, A kumkhua nangmah ta asi.

CHO:   Alleluia, Alleluia, Amen Alleluia, Thluasuahnak um hram seh.

2.Leitlun miphun hmuahhmuah tla in, Vanmi nang thawn lungkhat tein, Runtu Bawipa kan thangthat ding, Vansangbik ah na hmin thang seh.

3.In duhnak khuaruahhar asi, A fimnak le in duhnak dawtnak, Cherubim le Seraphim in, In duhdawtnakan sim thei lo.

4.Ra cio uh law Runtu Bawipa, Cawrawl ei nak kuang sung ah cun, A thleng ih Bawipa an it ter, In duhdawtnak ziang tluk atum.
