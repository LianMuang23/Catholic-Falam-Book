20.THIANGHLIM ZAN

Key-C Christmas Hla

1.Thianghlim zan, dai theh zan, Bethlehem khua lam ah, A it mi Naute lam ah, Maria le Joseph an um, (Vancung nunnuamnak khi)2

2.Thianghlim zan, dai theh zan, Tuu khal pawl tla hnen ah, Aw mawi thawn vancungmi pawl, Lungawinak thu hla an sa tlang, (Runtu Bawipa a suak zo)2

3.Thianghlim zan, dai theh zan, Tuukahal pawl khukbil in an um, (Kanmah hrang ah suak zo)2

4.Thianghlim zan, dai theh zan, Hruai Arsi tleunak in, Mifim pawl in an thlun tlang, Anmah ih thilri tla an sum, (Bawipa Jesuh hnenah)2
