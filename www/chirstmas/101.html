101.ZING ARSI A SUAK ZO

Key-C Missa Hlan Sakding Hla

1.Deirel Arsi khi a suak zo, Ni tlaknak in khua a vang zo, Vanrang tang Bawi Pathian par ah, Thianghlim hmin khi thangṭhat ding ah.

CHO:   Pathian hmai ah khuk bil uh la hla ṭha rem in thangṭhat uh law, Thawmtum nak le zaangfahnak khi, Tha nuamnak thawn kan thangṭhat ding.

2.Tuisun sung ih zonzaih har nak, Rak thiang ter aw harhdam thei nak, Thu ṭha zaangfahnak in pe aw, Kan sualnak hmuah in thian ter aw.

3.Pathian le Fa Pathian par ah, Thiang Thlarau Pathian hnen khal ah, A thawhtir ih a um bangin, Thangṭhatnak um seh kumkhua in.
