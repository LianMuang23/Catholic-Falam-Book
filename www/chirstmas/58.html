58.BAWIPA THUKHAM THAR

Key-F Thosal Hla

1.Bawipa Jesuh in kan hnen, Thukham a thar mi pakhat in pek, Keimah ih ka lo duh bang tuk in, Khat le khat duh aw uh tin in sim.

2.Hi bang in a sim hnu ah, Amah duhdawtnak theih ter dingah, A thlun tu pawl ke a kholh hnu ah, Duh awknak ding thukam thar a pek.

3.Maw u le nau hmuahhmuah tla, Bawipa ih duhnak ruat cio hnik uh, Thih tiang tuar in duhnak tumbik cu, Bawipa duhnak rul kir ding a har.

4.Bawipa thihnak in a tho, Sal hnu ah thlun tu tla hnen a lang, Thiang Thlarau ngah ding in thu a kam, Hnu ah Vancung a Pa hnen a kai.

5.A pek mi thu tla ruat in, Bawipa duhnak thlun ding cu a har, Amah duhnak sung um thei ding in, Duh awknak hmun ter ringring uh si.
