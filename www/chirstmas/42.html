42.ZAANGFAHNAK NEITU BAWI JESHU

Key-C Vua Hla
CHO:   Zaangfahnak nei tu Bawi Jesuh, Kan lawmnak cem thei lo, (Pathian thawn nunnuamnak tan in, Hi leitlun ah a tum.)2 Pa Pathian ih thinhengnak khi, Kan hnen in a luat ter, Ral cangtu minung pawl hrangah, Thihnak tiang a tuar zo.

2.Hi leitlun ah thatlonak tla, A tampi a tuar zo, Khuavang Bawi le minung pawl in, Zonzaihnak hi an pek.

3.Minung pawl in zaangfah hrangah, Zohsuamnak hmuah a tuar, Ral cangtu pawl runnak dingah, Thinglamtah a phur zo.

4.Kan tuah mi sualnak hmuahhmuah khi, Jesuh'n lak hlo theh, In zaangfah Bawi nangmah hnenah, Lawmnak thu um seh law.
