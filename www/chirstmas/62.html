62.JESUH CHRISTU TUIHSUN ATHO

Key-D Thosal Hla

1.Jesuh Christu tui sun atho Alleluia, Cuih hrangah kan biak tlang pei Alleluia, Voi khat thinglamtah par ah Alleluia, IN tlen dingin a tuar asi Alleluia.

2.Misual tla runnak dingin Alleluia, thinglamtah le phumnak tuar Alleluia, Christu kanmah Bawi hnen ah Alleluia, Lawmnak hla kan sak tlang pei Alleluia.

3.A tuarnak zonzaih tla in Alleluia, Kannih runnak kan ngah asi Alleluia, Vancungmi pawl ih ram ah Alleluia, A tu cu Siangpahrang asi Alleluia.

4.A ram cu a cem thei lo Alleluia, Pathian hnen hla kan sak pei Alleluia, Pa, Fa, Thiang Thlarau Pathian Alleluia, Vancung mi thawn rak lawm uh Alleluia/p>
