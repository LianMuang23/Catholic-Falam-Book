85.THIANGHLIM SACRAMENTU

Key-F Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.Thianghlim Sacramentu, Leitlun inn te a um, Na Thianghlim inn hmai ah, Upat zet in kan thleng, Jesuh duhnak le lawmnak hla, Na hnen ring zet in hla kan sak, (Thianghlim Sacramentu)2

2.Remnak Sacramentu, Thinlung za te hmuah in, Nuamnak ṭha le colh hmun, Riahsiatnak pawl hlo hmun, Na hna ah ka zonzaih thu tla, Rinsan zet in kan sim asi, (Remnak Sacramentu)2

3.Colhnak Sacramentu, Tipa thlitu lak in, In luat ter tu tibaw, Ti kap in thlen ter tu, Thlitu cu a cawl hrih lo ih, In kilkhawi in in luat ter aw, (Colhnak Sacramentu)2
