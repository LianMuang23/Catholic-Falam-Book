7.BETHLEHEM MIPUM SIARNAK

Key-G Christmas Hla

1.Bethlehem khapi sung ah cun, Leitlun mipum siar an tuah, Cunah remnak Bawi a rung suak, Lei mi ah an siar ding maw.

CHO :   An thangthatnak aw ngai uh, Vancung mi pawl hla mawi cu, Cungnungbik cu a hmin thang seh, Lei ah rem leng seh an ti.

2.Sualnak sungih um mi pawl le, Thih le lungawinak hrangah, Leitlun ah ni mawite a suak, Leitlun thimnak a tleu ter.

3.Rak tleu sal aw, Vancung Arsi, Van tleunak thawn kom ter aw, Aw tleu ter aw, Ka thangthatnak, Vancung khua sung thleng tiangin.
