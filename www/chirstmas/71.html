71.RUNTU BAWI A NUNG TI KA THEI

Key-D Thosal Hla

1.Ka thei in runtu Bawi a nung zo, Hi leitlun ah, in uk sal ding, Ka thei kumkhua nunnak in pek zo, Ka thi hman ah, ka nung sal ding.

CHO:   A si ka thei, Jesuh Khrih a nung zo, Hi leitlun ah in uk sal ding, A si ka thei, nunnak in pek zo, A thiannak vel a kut sungah.

2.Ka thei thukamnak a pelh lo ding, Thu in sim mi, a siat ve lo, Pumsa nunnak cu a cem cing ding, Amah tonnak, can rei lo ding.

3.Ka thei ka nunnak ding hmun a rem, Ka nunnak ding, ka um ve ding, Aw mangbangza in kil veng in hnem, Ka hnen khal ah, a ra cing ding.
