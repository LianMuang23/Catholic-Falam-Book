4.VAN IH AWTHAWG CU

Key-D Adventu Hla

1.Hramlak ih tlang au tu in, Bawipa lam kha sial uh law, A lam kawi kha ding ter in, A rat nak ralring cio uh.

CHO :   Ra aw, maw Bawipa, Kanmah thinlung sungah, Rak leng ve aw kan hnenah, Na hmel mawi in rak hmuh aw.

2.Leilung le a sung um mi, Nunnak nei mi hmuahhmuah in, Lungkim ten in tuah tu khi, Bawipa kan hngak kannih in.

3.Riahsiatnak dar aw thawng cun, Lawmnak Jubillee dar hi, A cang ding na rat ah cun, Kan hngak mi Jesuh na si.
