78.A ṬHA CUANGMI JESUH THINLUNG

Key-G Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.A ṭha cuang mi Jesuh thinlung, Bawipa nangmah kan lo duh, Bawipa nangmah kan duhnak cu, Kannih in kan hlon thei lo, Bawipa nangmah kan duhnak cu, Karh theinak ding thu in pek,(Sualnak in in luat ter ko aw, Kanmah tla in zaangfah aw)2

2.Bawipa nangmah kanmah tla thawn, Sunnihlawh kan hnaṭuan hmuah, Zan a thleng kan ihthat sungah, Nitin ten in rak kilkhawi, Khuavang in sualforh hman sehla, Kannih tla kan ṭih lo,(Kan thinlung sung ah um awla, Lam ṭha mi in hmuh sak aw)2

3.Bawipa na ke hram ah pan in, Bawmnak Zaangfahnak kan dil, Nitin in in rak zaangfah aw, Ṭhatlonak pial thei dingin, Zonzaih harsatnak lak ihsin, Thlacamnak hngilh lo ding in, (Sual sawn nak kan tong sual tikah, Aw Jesuh in rak run aw)2
