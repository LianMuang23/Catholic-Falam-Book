35.BAWI JESUH THINGLAMTAH PAR IH AN TAR LAIAH

Key-F Vua Hla

1.Kan Bawipa thinglamtah ah an tar lai ah, Hling lukhum le puansen an sin ter, Leilung tlun hmuah khuathim a tlung ih ling khal a hnin.

CHO:   Zoh aw, Calvary tlang par ah, Bawipa a thihnak riahsia in zoh aw, A pa vanram in tuah cia mi, Thlarau hrangah a thi zo.

2."Ka ti a hal," tiah a au thinglamtah in, Phaikuang in ti thur cu an hun pek, Zonzaih phunkim a tuar theh hnu ah fei in an sun.

3.Sun lai nazi hleihnih in zan pathum tiang, Riahsiatnak khuathim hmuah a tlung ih, Bawinu Maria le unau tla in an rak tah pi.

4."E lawi, E lawi" tiah a au a Pa hnen ah, "Ziang ah so i run tanta ka Pa," Tin a au theh in a lu kun in a thi asi.
