276.NA FA TE NGAI AW BAWI JESUH

Key-A Zanlam Hla

1.(Na fate ngai aw Bawi Jesuh, Kanmah zan thlacam lai ah, Ral hmuahhmuah lak in in run aw, In kil aw, in kilkhawi aw)2

2.(Khuavang sualsawmnak in run aw, Zan khua thim itthat lai ah, In kilkhawi tu vancung mi tla, A mit hmuh in kil seh law)2

3.(Jesuh zaangfah in in kil aw, Tlun na thawm na to hmun in, Zaan var te thin lung ralring aw, Duhnak Sacramentu sung)2

4.(Zan khuathim zamrang a kim ih, Tleunak in thimnak a cang, Kan lak ah thihnak a nai tikah, Inn ah na fa te hruai aw)2
