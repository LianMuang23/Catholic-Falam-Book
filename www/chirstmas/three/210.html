210.ZAANGFAHNAK PHUNDANG

Key-G Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.In zaangfahnak phundang asi, Kei mi sual in run zo, Kei mi hlo in hmu sal tiang in, Mit caw khua ka hmu zo.

2.Ralrin theinak zaangfahnak in, Ṭihnak cem ter asi, Ka zum tir in a tu tiang in, Ka hrang man khung asi.

3.Ṭihnak retheinak sung ihsin, In hruai suak zo asi, A tu tiang in a neh pi si, Vancung thleng ding kan si.

4.Vancungram ah a kumkhua in, Ni bang in ka tleu ding, Pathian laksawng hla mawi sak in, Kan nuam awk sinsin ding
