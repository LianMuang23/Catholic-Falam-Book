214.A CAAN REILO IN KAN TLUNG DING

Key-F Hnemnak Hla

1.Lungawinak hla kha sak cio uh si feh phah in, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding, Kumkhua in ni a tlang ding thimnak hmuah hlo in, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding.

CHO:   A caan rei lo in, a caan rei lo in, Thihnak tiva kan tan ding, Thlisia hrannak cem tikah, Kan tong aw cing ding, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding.

2.Kan ṭuanmi hnaṭuan kha zuamnak thawn kan ṭuan ding, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding, Nitin kan himding Bawi Pathian zaangfahnak in, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding.

3.A duhmi hrangah lamzin kha rem sak uh si, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding, Duhnak thinlung mi hmuahhmuah hnen ah nei uh si, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding.

4.Hnangamnak le colhnak a um khatlam ral ah, A rei hlan ah vancung kan tleng ding, Mithli tlaknak aum nawn lo khi khua sungah, A rei hlan ah vancung kan thleng ding.
