242.HLA THAR AN SAK ASI

Key-C Hnemnak Hla

1.Vancung inn sungah tlen cia pawl hla an sak, Runtu Siangpahrang a hmin kha thangṭhat in, Mithiang mi in lungawinak hla thar an sak, Hi leitlun retheinak hmuahhmuah kha tan in.

CHO:   Jesuh thangṭhat uh, Calvary tlang ah a thi zo, Jesuh thangṭhat uh, Thihnak neh in a tho sal zo.

2.In duhdawtnak hla mawi sak ding a kim zo, A zumtu in thluasuahnak kha an ngah ding, Runtu Bawi cu thihnak in a thosal zo, Pathian hnen ah a kumkhua in kan um ding.

3.Puanvar sin in a thiang cia mi pawl lawng in, A zaangfahnak in cuih hla thar an sak ding, A sual mi ngaithiam ding a zaangfahnak in, A kumkhua in lungawinak hla kan sak ding.
