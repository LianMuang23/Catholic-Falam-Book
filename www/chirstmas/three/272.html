272.LEITLUN KAN NUNNAK

Key-G Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Hi leilung tlun ih kan nunnak, Tinbaw to bang kan si, Thlisa tipi a let tikah tinbaw a siat asi, Aw Ave tipi Arsi pi khuasia a rat tikah, Kanmah in hruai in kil awla, Thu hman lam in hmuh aw.

CHO:   Duh ṭul bik mi kanmah Bawinu, Aw Maria, Kan nopnak Aw Bawinu, Kan nopnak, aw Bawinu, Vancungram vancungram nuam thleng thei dingin, Na fate pawl hmuahhmuah hrang ah, Thla in cam ringring aw.

2.A siat thei leitlun nunnuamnak, Thi le sa duhnak le, Sualforhnak pawl in naih tikah, Bawinu in kil awla, Kan kiang kap kho mi hling pawl, In kei i sun thei asi, Natnak tuar in ka bang tikah, Na kut in san hram aw.

3.Aw Bawinu thin bang mi kanmah na kut in run zap aw, Vancungram sui khuapi sungah, Daiten in cawl ter aw, Vancung mithianghlim bur pawl le Bawinu hminthannak khi, Thianghlim biakṭheng hmai ah bok in, In thangṭhat ter asi.
