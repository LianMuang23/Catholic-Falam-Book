252.VANCUNGMI IN MARIA HNENAH

Key-F Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Vancungmi in Maria hnen ah, Zaangfahnak thawn in na kim ṭheh, Thiangthlarau ih huham nak thawn in, Fapa Pathian na vun asi.

CHO:   Ni tin ten zing lam sun le zan lam, Ka nu nangmah hnen ah lawm nak, Vancungmi Gabriel ih ṭongmi, Kanmah khal kan hmang ding asi.

2.Kei cu Pathian ih sal nu ka si, Amah ih duhnak bang tuk in, Nangmah ih in sim mi hmuahhmuah cu, Keimah sal nu tlun ah cang seh.

3.Cuih tik cuu ah Pathian ih Ṭong cu, Milai ruangpumpi a lak ih, Kanmah minung hrang ah lungawinak, Bawipa kanmah hnen ah a ṭum.

4.Na Fapa Bawi Jesuh Khrih hnen ah, Kanmah hrang thla in cam sak aw, Kanmah par ah zaangfahnak pek ding, Vancungram ah thleng thei ding in.
