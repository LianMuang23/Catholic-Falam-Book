264.THAWM THAWN KIMTU NU MARIA

Key-D Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Thawm thawn kim tu kan Bawinu cu, Kumkhua ṭhatnak nei tu asi, Van le lei uk tu kan Bawinu, A sung ah nau a sem asi.

CHO:   Thawm tum nak nei Nu Maria, Kanmah hrang thla in cam sak aw, Thawm tum nak nei Nu Maria, Kanmah hrang thla in cam sak aw.

2.Nunau hmuah lak ah Bawinu khi, Bawinu nangmah sung ih um tu, Ziang kip ah a sunglawi asi, Ṭhatnak nei nau te Jesuh cu.

3.Cung Maria Pathian ih Nu, Na fa pawl kan thih cut tiangin, Thla in cam sak aw, Kanmaih Nu, Pathian ram co theinak dingin.
