204.KAWHNAK AW MAWI

Key-G Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Ngai uh Bawipa ih kawhnak aw mawi, Rundamnak thu sim dingah in ko, In zaangfahnak thu pawl sim dingin, A tu in sim thok ve thlang aw.

CHO:   Thawmtum Bawipa Alleluia, Hmun kip ah a thu cu va sim cio uh, Kan Bawipa le Jesuh ram a can hlan tiang, A zaangfahnak sunlawi ṭhatnak in, Hmun tin ram tin thang suak ko seh.

2.Leitlun tleu nan si, Thuthluntu hmuah, Kan Bawipa kan thangṭhat tlang nak ding, Nun-can ṭha le nan tleunak suah uh, Leitlun ram thim tleu ter dingin.

3.Leitlun ni le thla an tleu lai ah, Bawipa hrang hnu lo in ṭuan uh si, Bawipa fang pawl an khawm hlan ah hin, Zamrang ṭuan aw na hnu pang ding.
