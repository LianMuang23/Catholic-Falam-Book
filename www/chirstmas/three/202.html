202.BAWIPA THANGṬHATNAK HLA KAN SAN DING

Key-C Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Bawipa thangṭhat in hla ka sak ding, Ka hrang ah nunnak pe tu asi, Thinglamtah par ah tuar in in tlen tu asi, Miphun kip in lawm uh.

CHO:   (Lawm uh lawm cio uh)2Van Bawi Fa cu kan hnen a ra, A zaangfahnak cu in rak pek sal zo, Lawm uh Alleluia.

2.Thangṭhatnak le lawmnak na hnen kan pek, Aw duhdawt tu cahnak thar in pe aw, Na thluasuahnak kha kan hnen ah bur in, Cahnak thar in in thuam ve aw.

3.A hmin thianghlim in um seh, Pathian Fapa leilung sual tlen tu cu, Jesuh thawi nak Sacrament ih um, Ṭhatnak le Duhnak thawn in a khat.
