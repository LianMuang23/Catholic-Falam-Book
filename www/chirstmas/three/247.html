247.HMIN KAWH TIKAH

Key-G Hnemnak Hla

1.Bawi Jesuh a ṭum sal tikah mumek aw thawng kan thei ding, Kumkhua in tleunak ni bangin a tlang ding, Runnak a ngah cia mi hmuah thawn vancung ah kan tong aw ding, Hmin an kawh tikah keimah ka um ve ding.

CHO:   Vancungah hmin an kawh tikah, Vancungah hmin an kawh tikah, Vancungah hmin an kawh tikah, Hmin an kawh tikah, Keimah ka um ve ding.

2.A tleu bik in zingpit te in a thi mi an tho sal ding, Bawi Jesuh thawhsalnak sunglawi an co ding, Runnak a ngah mi a thianghlim vancung ah an khawm aw ding, Hmin an kawh tikah keimah ka um ve ding.

3.Zing in sun tiang kan Bawipa Jesuh hrang ah hna kan ṭuan ding, In zaangfahnak sunglawi thu khi kan sim ding, Hi leitlun nunnak a cem in kan ṭuan mi a cem ve ding, Hmin an kawh tikah, Keimah ka um ve ding.
