209.VANBAWI SUNGLAWI ZIANGHMUAH TUANTU

Key-G Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Van Bawi tumbik ziang hmuah tuah tu Pathian, Maria fa Jesuh Pathian a cang, Vanmi le mi thianghlim in an thangṭhat, An lawmnak hla mawi kha a zam lai ah.

CHO:   Vansangbik hmun lam ah khin Hosana, Thianghlim thianghlim lei le van Bawipa, A miphun hrang ih nunnak pe tu cu, Alleluia Alleluia Amen.

2.Bawi tumbik ziang hmuah tuah thei tu biak in, Mi hmuah a hmai ah an khukbilnak ding, Leilung in a sunglawinak khi phuang seh, A uai thei lo mi duhdawtnak famkim.

3.Kan hrangah vancung laksawng sunglawi bik, Van Manaa amah ih thlumnak nei mi, Vanmi pawl in an run ret cabuai ah, Anmah pawl thlun in thinlung a nuam ee.

4.Thih hnu thosal famkimnak thawn khat mi, Kumkhua ih nungmi dangnak nei lo mi, Ṭhen thei lo zianghmuah ih kumkhua famkim, Van Bawi tumbik zianghmuah tuahtu Pathian.
