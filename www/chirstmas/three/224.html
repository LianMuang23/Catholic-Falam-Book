224.RUNTU BAWIBIK RUNG TUM AW

Key-C Hnemnak Hla

1.Ka ṭahnak aw ngai in i run zaangfah, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum, Ka thlarau in a se herh nangmah kha, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum.

CHO:   Inn hlat pi in tlang le fing kha kaan in, Keimah cu lam palh in ka um, I run sang in i thlun ter na hnen ah, Runtu Bawibik rung ṭum.

2.Zaan thim sungah beu nak hmun ka nei lo, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum, Nun tleunak ding ah nangmah na um ko, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum.

3.Ka um har in a feh ka bang ngaingai, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum, Ka thlir vel ih a thlam nangamai mithmai, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum.

4.In taan siang hlah thinlung rethei zonzai, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum, Aw kaa nem in ka thlacamnak run ngai, Runtu Bawi bik rung ṭum.
