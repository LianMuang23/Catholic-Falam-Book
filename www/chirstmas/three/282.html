282.BAWI JESUH IN THIT AWK NAK A THANGTHAT

Key-A Thitumnak Hla

1.Hlan ah Galilee ram Kana khua ah, Nupi ṭhitpuai na thangṭhat pinak thu kan thei, Tu ah kan unau te neih awknak hi, Thangṭhatnak thawn in lungawi pi aw.

CHO:   Na hnen kan dil zaangfahnak in run bawm aw, Nun sung thil phunkip ah nangmah in bawm hram aw, An nunnak ni a cem hlan sung hmuah in, A kumkhua an hnen ah um pi aw.

2.An duhawknak tam sinsin thei dingin, An nunsung harnak hmuah lak in, Nangmah hruai aw, Fanu fapa neihnak run zaangfah aw, Na zaangfahnak thawn, in bawm hram aw.

3.Thukham an tuahnak cabuai hmai ah hin, Kumkhua ṭhen aw lo ding in, An thukam mi cu, Theih pi ve in, Nangmah in lawm ve aw, Thianghlim te in nunnak run pe aw.
