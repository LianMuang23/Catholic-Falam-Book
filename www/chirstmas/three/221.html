221.RUNTU IN I HRUAI DING

Key-G Hnemnak Hla

1.Runtu Bawipa in kumkhua i hruai ding, Sualforh bumnak sung in kei i hruai ding, Thluasuah pek in a hnen ah in hruai ding, A hnen ah ka um ve ding.

CHO:   A tuah cia mi ram nuam ah, In hruai ding ram mawi ah cun, A kumkhua nunnuamnak co ve ding in, In hruai ding kan Pa ram ah.

2.Lei harnak thihnak ram kan taan hlan sung, Riahsiatnak in a tuam sual ral a tam, Cem thei lo a kumkhua nunnak ram ih, Len ding cu ka hngak hlap ee.

3.Vanram sunglawi hmun kan thleng tikah cun, Runtu Bawi thangṭhatnak hla kan sak ding, Vanmi thianghlim pawl thawn lung rual te in, Kumkhua in kan nuam tlang ding.
