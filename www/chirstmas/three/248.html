248.PATHIAN KUT SUNGAH

Key-C Hnemnak Hla

1.Runtu Bawi in keimah i hruai tikah, Rethei zonzaih thleng hman seh ka him ding, Thlisia tilet thlen tikah i hum ding, A kut thawmlian pi sungah.

CHO:   A kut thawm lian, pi sungah, A kut thawm lian, pi sungah, Rethei zonzaih thleng hman seh ka him ding, A kut thawmlian pi sungah.

2.In duhdawtnak ih a ṭang ah in cawi ding, A thluasuah daihnak le thu ṭha in sim, Pathian kilven nak in kanmah kan him, A kut thawmlian pi sungah.

3.Kan thlarau tan lo in a kilkhawi ding, In duhnak thu cu dai ti um lo ding, A tu le a kumkhua in kilkhawi ding, A kut thawm lian pi sungah.
