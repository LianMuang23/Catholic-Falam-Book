223.BAWIPA TUAH CIA EDEN THAR

Key-D Hnemnak Hla

1.Bawipa tuah cia Eden thar cu, Pan cio uh law rual duh hmuah in, Vanmi thiang le Seraphim thawn, Lawmnak par rim hmui kha an tlan.

CHO:   Aw ziang tikah ka thleng ve ding, Bawipa tuah cia ram thar ah cun, Rualpi pawl le van mi pawl thawn, Ka hngak hlap lungrual ih len ding.

2.Ka hngak hlap tuk aw ka Bawipa, Kumkhua ramnuam ih len ding cu, I hruai tluan aw ka Bawi nunnem, Kumkhua ramnuam ka thlen tiang in.

3.Kumkhua ramnuam ka thlen tikah, Leitlun harnak ramsung ah hin, Vakvai pawl kan colhnak hmunnuam, Run thleng ter aw ka Bawi nunnem.
