265.THIANGHLIM BAWINU

Key-F Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Thianghlim Bawinu na hmai khukbil, Pathian ih thianghlim bik Nu, Kanmah in kan lo duh bangin, Na fa cang ding in tuah aw.

CHO:   Khat le khat duh ding le bawm ding in, Kan nunnak man nei ding in, Sual hmuahhmuah kan nehnak dingin, In zirh aw thianghlim bik Nu.

2.Minung fate lak ah tum sawn, Na hnen ih naute pek mi, Leitlun pi tuah salnak dingin, Vancung in a ṭum asi.

3.Cu hmuahhmuah tuah tu Bawipa khi, Fala nangmah na cawi zo, Nangmah tumbik a tuah ding in, Nangmah thawn Jesuh a peh.

4.Na hmin in nunnak in pek aw, Ral kut sung in in run aw, A kumkhua in in zaangfah aw, In bawm aw vancung Bawinu.
