287.ZAANGFAH LE NGAIDAM THEI BAWIPA

Key-G Inn Thluasuah Peknak Hla

1.Ngaidam le zaangfah thei aw Bawipa, Cahnak thazaang cu in pek ruangah, Kan inn sak thar ah hin rung leng aw, Thluasuahnak in run pek hram aw.

CHO:   Bawipa kan inn hi na hrang kan sum, Na duh zawng hnaṭuannak hmun rak siseh, Sualnak le tukforhnak lak ih sin, Nang in rak thianghlim ter aw.

2.Nang cu kan himnak kulh pi na si, Cuih hrang ah ṭeek, rial, meikang, thlipi, Le misual ṭihnungza lak ih sin, Kilkhawi in in hum ringring aw.

3.Kan nunnak hi hman daan kan thiam lo, Hmai lam caan ih kan ton dingmi pawl, Kan ruah ban lo kei minung pawl in, Cuih hrangah in hruai camcin aw.

4.Nazareth innsang thianghlim bangin, Na umnak tlak mi si ve dingin, Hi innsungsang nun daan kaihruai aw, Thluasuah pek in thianghlim ter aw.
