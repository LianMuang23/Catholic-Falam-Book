245.PATHIAN UKNAK A NAI

Key-A Hnemnak Hla

1.A sunglawi mi vancungram Pathian uknak in in hngak lai, A kumkhua in a thiangmi pawl pumkhawm in, Ni le thla ni tin liam bangin Pathian uknak a nai sin, Vancung ram ah liannak ni kha a nai sin.

CHO:   Vancungram ah liannak ni kha a nai sin, Vancungram ah liannak ni kha a nai sin, Maw ka thlarau timcia in khawmnak mumek an tum ding, Siangpahrang sunlawinak thawn a thleng ding.

2.Khi vancung uknak ding le sunlawinak hmuah tim cia in, A thleng ding mi kan Bawipa khi va thlir uh, In run zo ih leitlun sualnak le, Sal cannak sung ih sin, Vancungram a liannak ni thla a nai sin.

3.Vancungram a liannak ni a kim tikah Pathian kan hmu ding, Khi caan hlan ah uknak ni lang hmaisa ding, Alleluia in tlentu hmin thangṭhat uh thi thlet mi in, Vancungram ah liannak ni thla a nai sin.

4.Kum saupi tiang hngak tu hi leitlun mi van a sang sinsin, A rei tikah mi thinlung a der ngaingai, Vancungram thu sim in mi.
