281.NUPI THITNAK SACRAMENT

Key-D Thitumnak Hla

1.Lungawinak ni tik cu caan ṭha, Pathian hmai ah kan lungawinak kha pek uhsi, Leitlun kan nun sung hna ṭha ṭuan dingin, Nupi ṭhitnak Sacrament zum uh.

CHO:   A mawi bik vancung ah kan thleng tikah, Kan thlarau a tleu ter tu Runtu Jesuh hmai ah, Vancung mithianghlim tla thawn kan leng ding, Lungawi zet hngin in sui lukhum thawn.

2.Ka nun sungah thinlung khat in, Bawi Jesuh ih a tuah mi nupi ṭhitnak cu, Vancung khua tiang in kan thlen theinak ah, Hngilh lo nak dingin zuam hram uh si.

3.Hlan ah Adam le Eva in, Pathian laksawng ṭhabik nunnak an hlo hrang ah, Bawi Jesuh in Nu le Pa thit awt nak, Thianghlim thei nak dingin in sim zo.

4.A nuam bik le a mawi bik mi, Pathian laksawng sunglawi nunnak cu kan hmuh ih, Cem thei lo pangpar rim hmui bangtukin, Kumkhua cat lo duhnak hmun hram seh.
