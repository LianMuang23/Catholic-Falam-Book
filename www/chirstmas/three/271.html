271.MARIA VANCUNGRAM AH

Key-C Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Tu ni ah vancungram nuam pi ah, Bawinu Maria an hruai asi, Huham thawn to in tokham tlun ah, Vancung Siangpahrang a cang asi.

CHO:   Maria cu vancungram ah, Hmin thannak hmuah thawn a kim asi, Kanmah hrang ah Fapa hnen ah, Zaangfah le bawmnak in dil sak aw.

2.Pa Pathian fanu aw Maria, Thawmtumnak thawn na kim asi, I kim ter aw Pa duhnak sungah, Na fa le pawl in kilkhawi asi.

3.Maria Bawipa Jesuh Nu cu, A Fapa hnen a um ringring, Amah thawn um khawm theinak ding cu, Na thlacamnak thawng in kan thleng ding.

4.Vancungram um mi mithianghlim pawl, Siangpahrang Bawinu thangṭhat uh, Suah pi u nau le rual pi pawl, A thianghlim mi Bawinu thangṭhat uh.
