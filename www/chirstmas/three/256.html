256.NITIN TEN MARIA HNENAH

Key-A Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Ni tin te in Maria hnen ah, Thangṭhatnak hla mawi sak in, A puai tin ah thinlung tak ten, Thlacam in thangṭhat uh si.A thawmtumnak ruah tik ah, Kan thei lo mangbangza asi, Kanmah ih Nu Fala Bawinu, Miṭhabik Nu Maria.

2.Duh le rinsan in pan uh si, Kan Bawinu in in bawm ding, Thlisia tisuar a um tikah, Amah in a dai ter ding, Vancung sunlawinak in pek zo, Kan Siangpahrang Bawinu in, Pathian khua tleu zaangfahnak thawn, A hnaṭuan pawl bawmtu Nu.

3.Bawinu'n kan thinlung in nuam ter, Amah thangṭhat hla kan sak, Kan Siangpahrang Bawipa ih Nu, Thianghlim Fala Maria, A thawmliannak a hmin thannak, Zohman an ban dah lo ding, Kan thinlung ih sin duhdawtnak, Bawinu zamrang pek uh si.
