217.SIANGPAHRANG HMAIAH DING IN

Key-A Hnemnak Hla

1.Siangpahrang hmai ah ding in, Vanmi thawn hla kan sak ding, Tui hnu ah, tui hnu ah, A tleu mi sui lamzin in Bawipa kha kan thangṭhat ding, Tui hnu ah, tui hnu ah.

CHO:   Siangpahrang hmai ah ding in, Vanmi thawn hla kan sak ding, A hmin ṭha kan thangṭhat ding, Alleluia, Alleluia, Siangpahrang hmai ah ding in.

2.Vancung darkhing kha tum uh, Siangpahrang hmai ah ding in, Tui hnu ah, tui hnu ah, A tleu mi sui lamzin in, Bawipa kha kan thangṭhat ding, Tuih hnu ah, tui hnu ah.

3.Kan thlarau a tu tho uh, Siangpahrang hmai ah ding in, Tui hnu ah, tui hnu ah, Nan neih mi Pathian pek uh, Amah hmui hmel bang ding in, Tui hnu ah, tui hnu ah.
