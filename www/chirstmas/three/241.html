241.SANGKAA RAK ONG UH

Key-E Hnemnak Hla

1.Jesuh kawhnak aw ngai uh, Rethei mi ra uh a ti, Amah lawng runtu asi, Sangkaa rak ong uh.

CHO:   Sangkaa rak ong uh, Sangkaa rak ong uh, Amah lawng runtu asi, Sangkaa rak ong uh.

2.Runtu Bawipa a ṭum zo, A nunnak nan hrang a liam, Thlan thim sung in a tho zo, Sangkaa rak ong uh.

3.Jesuh cu nanmah hngak in, A hnen thleng tiang in feh uh, Amah thawn lungawi khat in, Sangkaa rak ong uh.

4.Jesuh hnen ah ra cio uh, Runnak ding caan a nai zo, A caan kha luan hlan ah, Sangkaa rak ong uh.
