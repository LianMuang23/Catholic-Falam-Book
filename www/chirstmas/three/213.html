213.THIANGHLIM PATHIAN HMIN LAWM UH

Key-F Thangṭhatnak Hla

1.Thianghlim Pathian hmin lawm uh, Kan zaten na hmai ah kun in, Leitlun za ten na kut hnuai, Vancungmi za ten an biak ih, (Na uknak khi thim thei lo, Na hmin cu a kumkhua in)2

2.Rak ngai uh vancung hla rem, Vancungmi khal in an sak ih, Cherubim le Seraphim, Catlo aw khat in an lawm tlang, (Aw rem thawn vancung tiang in, Thianghlim, thianghlim, thianghlim Bawi)2

3.Cuih hnu ah a thluntu tla, Na thianghlim hmin an lawm tlang ih, Prophet tla aw ring zet in, Thianghlim mi tla thawn an thlun tlang, (Zing pit in Ni tlak tiang in Biakinn sung ah an sak tlang)2

4.Thianghlim Pathian, Pa, Fapa, Thiang Thlarau pathum kan zum in, A hram ah cun pakhat lawng, Ṭhen thei lo Pathian kan zum, (Thuthuk khi zum tlang zet in, Kan lo biak khukbil tlang in)2
