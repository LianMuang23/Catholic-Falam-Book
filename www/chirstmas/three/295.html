295.BAWI JESUH KHRIH PAWLPI

Key-F Hla Phunphun

1.Hi leitlun ram hmuahhmuah ah Bawi Jesuh Khrih Pawlpi cu miphun kip in an duhduh in an biak, Sihmanseh runtu Bawi in Pawlpi pakhat lawng a tuah, Cuih Pawlpi cu thlarau nunnak asi.

CHO:   A tuah mi biaknak ah a tu zamrang in ra aw, Kumkhua ih nunnak cu dil ding in amaih hnen kan naih ding.

2.Tukforh sual sawmnak tuar in, In runtu Jesuh Khrih cu, Zonzai a tuar ṭheh in van ah a kai, Cuih hnu ah a Pawlpi cu, Ramkip ah harnak an tuar, Cubang tuk in harnak kan tuar a ṭul.

3.Jesuh in keimah duh tu, Ka thu thlun tu pawl tla cu, Harnak le zonzaihnak phunkim tuar in, An neih mi thilri tla le, Unau sungkhat tanta in, Keimah ka dung in i thlun seh a ti.
