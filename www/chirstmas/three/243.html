243.THINGLAMTAH RALKAP

Key-G Hnemnak Hla

1.Thinglamtah ralkap hmuahhmuah in, Jesuh hmin rinsan in feh uh si, Zoh uh khuasia pawl dung lam in, Kanmah siatsuah an duh.

CHO:   Nan thinglamtah cuthlir uh, Pathian thangṭhatnak hla sak in, Bawipa lungawinak sungah kan feh ding, Vancungram kan thlen hlan tiang.

2.Hmailam feh uh rual duh tla hmuah, Pathian hmai ah lawmnak hla sak uh, Kanmah pawl tla thawn hmun khat in, Van ah kan tong aw ding.

3.Vancungram ṭha kan thleng tikah, Kan Pa Pathian hmai kan hmu tlang ding, Ruangpum riahsiat mitthli tlaknak, Kan hngilh ṭheh cio leh ding.

4.Vancungram sung nunnuamnak cu, Cerh tidai bangin a luang ringring, Mi thianghlim pawl tla hmun khat in, Ni bang an tleu ringring.
