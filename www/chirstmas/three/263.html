263.VANCUNGRAM LAM IN HRUAI AW

Key-G Bawinu Maria Hla

1.Vancungram lam in hruai aw, Arsi bang in tleu Nu, Pathian khua var hmu duh pawl, Sualnak lak in run aw.

CHO:   Kan Nu, Aw Maria na duh na fate, A kumkhua in kilkhawi aw, Kanmaih Bawinu, Kan Siangpahrang, Bawinu laitlang ah um seh, Kanmaih hrang thla in cam aw, Bawinu Kan Nu.

2.Laitlang ram ah lawki pawl, Khuavang kut sung an um, An mah Nu na si hrangah, Pathian hnen ah hruai aw.

3.Pawlpi karh thei nak dingin, Tlangbawi tla cang ter aw, Rawl khawm tu tla a mal tuk, Kan Nu in zaangfah aw.

4.Khua hmuahhmuah ah thu zirh in, Pathian lam sial thei ding, Zumnak tumbik nei tu tla hril aw, Kanmai Bawinu.
