40.IN RAK RUNTU CALVARY AH

Key-G Vua Hla

1.In rak runtu, Calvary ah Thihnak naa bik a tuar, Leitlun mi tla in duh ruangah, A lu hlo ding a siang.

2.Misual pawl kha thiang ter dingah, A thisen a luang ter, Kumkhua har in in duhdawtnak, Sim cawk ding asi lo.

3.Lei le van tiang in an hram cio, Runtu Bawi Khrih thihnak, Thinglamtah thlainak zoh ngam lo, Bawipa na tuarnak cu.

4.Sualsir mitthli rul cawk lo mi, Na duhnak rul cawk lo, Kan thinlung le kan duhnak hmuah, Bawipa kan rul cawk lo.
