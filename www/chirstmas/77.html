77.AW THIANG THLARAU PATHIAN

Key-F
Thiangthlarau Pathian Hla

CHO:   Aw thianghlim Thlarau Pathian, Keimah i kilkhawi dingin, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

1.Thu hman nak i thei ter aw, Thinlung fimnak i pe aw, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

2.Na thuhla i thei ter aw, Thinlung tleunak i pek aw, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

3.Ruahthiamnak i run pek aw, Sual lo dingin i run aw, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

4.Sualnak lak in i run aw, Thinkhohnak i zaangfah aw, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

5.Fimthiamnak nei tu Bawipa, Fimnak i zirh aw Bawipa, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

6.Ṭha ngai te in zuamnak ding, Pathian thu ka thlunnak ding, Vancungram in rung ṭum.

7.Ṭuannak ṭha duhnak dingin, Pathian ṭihnak i zaangfah, Vancungram in rung ṭum.
