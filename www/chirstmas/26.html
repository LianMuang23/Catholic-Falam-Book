26.BAWIPA THIHNAK CALVARY AH

Key-C Vua Hla

1.Bawipa thihnak Calvary ah harsa zet in, a tuar nak cu, Kan sim thei lo in duhnak cu, A thisen a luan lai fang ah.

2.Fei sun in thihnak ih hlei ah, Thir khennak le hling lukhuh thawn, Ka Pa, ti in a au riangro, Van Bawi Fa thihnak a naa tuk.

3.Thinglamtah ih an thah lai ah, Vancungmi in thi an rung dong, Leitlun sualnak thian ter dingah, A thi luannak Calvary ah.

4.Kan ruat thei lo, Van Bawi thihnak, Misual kut in an thah lai ah, Maria le a rualpi pawl in, A zun ngai zet in an tah pi.

5.Vancung khua tha ah kan lut ding, A hmai lu kun in kan biak ding, Hla mawi sa in sui tingtang thawn, Nuam zet in ziang tik kan um ding.
