84.A THIANGHLIM BAWIPA THINLUNG

Key- Jesuh Thianghlim Thinlung Hla

1.{A thianghlim bik Bawipa, Thinlung ziangtik hman hngilh lo ding, Bawipa na har sat nak sung ah, Kannih kan tuar pi ding.}2

CHO:   Aw Bawipa, kei nangmah lo cun, Ziang tin so ka nung ding, Na thinlung in in duhnak mei ka tlun khal ah um ter aw.

2.{A sunglawi na thianghlim thinlung, Duhdawtnak thawn a khat, Na duhdawt bang thinlung a nei, Khui khal ah ka hmu lo.}2

3.{Bawipa na hnen feh ka duh naan, Ka thinlung a nuam lo, Ka thin nuam ding in tuah sak aw, Thianghlim Thinlung Pathian.}2
