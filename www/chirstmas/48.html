48.THIANNAK THI

Key- Vua Hla

1.Zoh uh kan sualnak, phurtu cu feh in, Thihnak Calvary a thleng zo, Thinglamtah nak le thihnak kha tuar in, Ka hrangah a thi tiang a luang zo.

CHO:   In Runtu Bawipa hngak sungin, A thisen le ti a suak zo, Ka lung sungah aum camcin, Sualnak a thian ter zo.

2.In zaangfahnak thi thiang cu a luang zo, Runtu Bawi a thihnak hmun in, A kumkhua in khi hmun ah va kholh aw, Thlarau runtu Bawipa hnen ah hin.

3.A thi luangmi in mi hmuah a kholh zo, An sualnak hmuah vur bang thiang in, Ka sualnak hmuah cu i thian ter ve zo, Ka hnen ra uh ati Bawipa in.

4.Sualnak lak ah ka vak nawn lo ding, In run ding Bawipa Jesuh in, A thiangbik mi Thlarau in i hruai ding, A sunlawinak ram kha hmu ding in.
