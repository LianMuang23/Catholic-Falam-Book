53.A HRANG NUNNAK KA PEK

Key-A Thosal Hla

1.Profet sim cia bangin Bawi Khrih cu, Bethlehem khua ah a rung suak ih, Kan hrang tuar in Calvary tlang Cross par ah, in run dingin tihnungza thihnak a tuar.

CHO:   A tho sal zo, Thanthat cio uh, Calvary thi mi cu, A umnak ih um ve dingin, A hrang nunnak ka pek.

2.Kan thinlung hmuah Bawipa hrang kan sum, Calvary ih in tlentu Bawi cun, In hruai dingin a rung tum sal tikah cun, Zumtu hmuah in lungawi in hla kan sak ding.

3.Alleluia kan lo lawm Bawipa, Duhdawt ruang ih thihnak a tuar cu, Thinglamtah nak tuar in in tlen suak theh zo, In runtu Bawipa thangthatnak hla kan sak.
