72.PATHIAN IN A PAWLPI

Key-G Thiangthlarau Pathian Hla

1.Pathian in a Pawlpi a din hram thok lai ah, Thlarau Thianghlim mei bangin a rung lang ih, An lu tlun ah fu in a then darh aw theh ih,Thlarau Thianghlim Christu thukham a rung thleng.

CHO:   Tuah thlang uh law, Timtuah thlang uh law, Zaangfahnak lam on lai ah, Timtuah thlang uh law, Timtuah thlang uh law, Zaangfahnak lam on lai ah.

2.Pathian cu thil ti theinak bangin a tlang theu, Mipi thawng tampi pawl an finkhawm aw ih, Thuthang tha nunnak, tong phunkim in an thei, Pathian Zaangfahnak lampi cu a ong zo.

3.A dungthlun pawl in Bawipa'i thucah cu phuang in, Duhdawtnak Thianghlim thawn an fingkhawi aw ih, Kholh fai in an um ih, Vur hnak in var sawn in, Thlarau Thianghlim cun kumkhua in a um pi.
